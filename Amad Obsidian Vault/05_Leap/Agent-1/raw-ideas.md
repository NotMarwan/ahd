# 💥 Agent 1 — Divergent Set · Agentic Money
> Gut checks 1–5: **S**=surprise ("never seen this"), **E**=emotion, **F**=72h-feasibility. Kill-list & Shariah-clean enforced.

## 🗑️ The 3 "safe" ideas — discarded as the price of entry
- **S1. "AI that auto-pays your bills"** — a Cleo clone. Feature, not category. Zero surprise. ✗
- **S2. "AI budgeting agent that sweeps money to savings"** — Cleo/Namaa-adjacent; optimization framing the West already owns. ✗
- **S3. "Chatbot that can also transfer money"** — an assistant with a button; drifts toward retired Rushd. ✗
> The gold is past these: not *optimization*, but **fulfilling obligations**; not an *assistant*, but a **wakala-governed agent that acts**.
