# يُسر · Yusr — the anti-riba
> *Yusr (يُسر)* = ease/relief — *"فإن مع العسر يُسرا"*. And the hadith: *"whoever relieves a believer's hardship, Allah relieves his on the Day of Judgment."*

## A. One-liner
**A bank-facilitated qard-hasan network where members fund a recycling, interest-free benevolent pool — AI matches a verified person in hardship to a zero-interest loan, their repayment lifts the next person, and a giver can convert any loan to outright sadaqah.** A bank whose product is mercy, not interest.

## B. Problem
Saudis carry a religious duty to lift each other interest-free (qard hasan) — but there's **no trusted, scalable way** to do it; people in a cash crunch fall into **riba credit-card debt** instead. Qard-hasan platforms abroad are nascent; **no bank-grade, AI-matched, recycling network exists** in KSA. **Why now:** OB can verify genuine need + affordability; Nafath establishes identity/trust; ALLaM triages requests; the licensed rails move money instantly.

## C. Solution
1. **Give into the pool** — members + the bank (CSR/zakat-eligible seed) contribute to a revolving **qard-hasan fund**.
2. **AI-matched relief** — someone in verified hardship (medical bill, a riba-debt rescue, an emergency) requests; AI checks genuine need + realistic repayment (OB) and issues a **0% loan** (principal only — no interest, fee, or markup, ever).
3. **Recycle** — repayments flow back to lift the next person; a giver can, anytime, **convert their share of a loan into sadaqah** (forgive it).
4. **See the chain** — "your SAR 1,000 has now helped 4 people in a year." Mutual aid you can watch.
- **Only-now/here:** AI need-verification + recycling at scale makes the oldest Islamic finance (qard hasan = the literal opposite of riba) finally work as a product. Only an Islamic bank would build the anti-interest loan.

## D. Mapping
- **Track:** 2 — Customer Experience. **Requirements:** **/03 CX** + **/04 Sustainability** (a self-replenishing relief fund) + **/02 AI** (need/affordability matching + triage).

## E. 72h build plan
- **Built:** the pool + give flow, the **AI need-matching + 0% loan issuance**, the recycling ledger, the "convert-to-sadaqah" action, the impact-chain view. **Mocked:** real disbursement rails, KYC, the fund's legal vehicle.
- **Must-work feature:** request → **AI verifies need → 0% loan issued → repayment recycles to the next person**, visible on the chain.

## F. Data story
Live: the pool balance, a request scored on genuine-need + affordability signals (OB), the **recycling multiplier** ("1 riyal given = N people helped over time"), and the riba-debt-avoided figure. The multiplier is the insight that makes a recycling fund feel infinite.

## G. Demo script (3 min)
1. "A bank's whole business is interest. What if a bank built the *opposite*?" 
2. Give SAR 1,000 to the pool. 
3. "Khalid" requests SAR 3,000 to clear a riba credit-card debt → AI verifies need + ability → **0% loan issued**. 
4. Months later his repayment **auto-funds the next person**; you tap "forgive my share → sadaqah." 
5. Close: "Your one gift, recycling forever, rescuing people from riba — *this* is what an Islamic bank is *for*."

## H. Alinma-ship case (uncontested)
**Qard hasan is the purest, most uncontested Islamic finance** — zero riba by definition. Not a profit center but a **brand + loyalty + CSR + acquisition** engine (the deepest trust an Islamic bank can earn), seedable with **zakat/CSR** funds and member giving; the bank earns the trusted-operator role + relationships, not interest. Advances financial inclusion + Vision-2030 social goals. PDPL/SAMA: structure as benevolent social finance (not a commercial credit product) → clean, and explicitly **off the retired credit kill-list**.

## I. Differentiation (incl. kill-list)
vs nascent qard-hasan apps → bank-grade trust + AI matching + **recycling** + convert-to-sadaqah. vs the retired Tadfuq/credit work → **0% benevolent, charity-framed, not a credit product** (the opposite of working-capital lending). vs Athar/Wasiyyah → a *different deed* (lift each other now vs give forever / leave rightly).

## J. Risks
| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Business model** — a bank making 0% loans | Frame as CSR/loyalty + zakat-seeded social fund + the recycling pool (self-sustaining principal); not pitched as a profit line. *Honest: this is the weakest leg.* |
| Default / moral hazard | AI affordability check + community accountability + convert-to-sadaqah absorbs losses by design; small caps (SAR 1–10k). |
| "Is it really a loan or charity?" | Clear qard-hasan structure (principal-only, repayment expected, forgiveness optional) — board-reviewed. |
| Demo realism | Seeded deterministic pool + recycling animation; offline fallback. |
