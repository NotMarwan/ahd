# 🃏 Wildcard (cross-lane) — هَدْي Hady
> Out of Lane 3, into **Lane 2 (Invisible Saudis)**. ⚠️ Overlaps Agent 2's pilgrim lane — **A2 leads pilgrims**; this is my one cross-lane bet, offered for portfolio range, not to compete.

## One-liner
**A faith-finance companion for the millions of foreign Hajj/Umrah pilgrims — multi-currency wallet, transparent ritual payments (hady/udhiyah sacrifice, fidyah), verified sadaqah at the holy sites, and scam protection — turning a spiritually peak, financially chaotic, fraud-exposed week into calm, dignified, halal money.**

## Why it could leap
- **Uniquely Saudi:** no other country hosts this. Millions arrive seasonally, in unfamiliar currency, targeted by fraud, needing to pay for **rituals** (the hady sacrifice is a religious requirement with a real, opaque payment flow) and wanting to give sadaqah at the most blessed sites of their lives. **Almost nobody builds for the pilgrim as a financial human.**
- **Faith-positive:** it serves *worship itself* — the sacrifice, the charity, the peace of mind to focus on ibadah. Shariah-clean by design (wallet + transparent ritual payments + verified charity; no contested contract).
- **Frontier × reality:** Nafath/Absher + Nusuk (the official Hajj platform) identity, multi-currency rails, AI scam-warnings in the pilgrim's language, verified hady/charity fulfillment on-chain-style receipts.

## Track + requirements
- **Track:** 2 — Customer Experience. **Requirements:** /03 CX + /02 AI + /04 Sustainability (recurring seasonal inclusion).

## 72h demo sketch
A pilgrim lands → wallet in their currency → pays for the **hady** and sees a verified receipt ("your sacrifice was performed + distributed to N families") → gives sadaqah at the Haram with verified impact → an AI flags a fake-campaign scam. The verified-ritual receipt is the wow.

## Why parked (not a finalist)
Collides with A2's owned lane; my faith-positive trio (Athar/Wasiyyah/Yusr) is the stronger, conflict-free contribution. Offered so the operator has a uniquely-Saudi, emotionally-rich option spanning faith × the most invisible high-volume segment.
