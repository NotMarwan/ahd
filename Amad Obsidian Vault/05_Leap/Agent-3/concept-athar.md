# 👑 أثر · Athar — sadaqah jariyah, as a product
> *Athar (أثر)* = the lasting **trace** you leave behind. Double meaning: your *impact* + your *legacy*.

## A. One-liner
**Athar turns any amount into a perpetual personal waqf — you give once, the principal is preserved forever in a halal endowment fund, and its returns give to your chosen cause for the rest of time, even after you're gone.** A donation gives once; an Athar gives forever.

## B. Problem (the human truth)
- The Prophet ﷺ named only **three** deeds that outlive a person — the first is **sadaqah jariyah** (ongoing charity). Every practicing Saudi wants one. **No consumer product lets an ordinary person create one.** You can *donate* (Ehsan: SAR 9bn+, but every riyal is spent once) — you cannot *endow*.
- **Waqf is "the sleeping giant of Islamic finance"** (~US$1T, tech-starved) and has always been **only for the wealthy** — you endow a building or a farm. The ordinary believer is locked out of Islam's most-rewarded act of giving.
- **Why now:** cash-waqf + **tokenized/fractional halal income funds** (ADIB Smart Sukuk, the waqf-ETF) finally make a *micro*-endowment viable; AWQAF's **digital endowment certificate** (Jood, Feb-2026) proves the registration rails exist; ALLaM + OB make it personal and effortless. The lock just became pickable.

## C. Solution
A 60-second flow that creates a real, perpetual endowment:
1. **Choose a cause** (orphans · water · a student's tuition · your local masjid · a verified Ehsan beneficiary).
2. **Endow an amount** (from SAR 100 — fund it from your account or round-ups via Open Banking).
3. Athar parks the **principal in an equity-based halal endowment fund** (AWQAF-style; no Tawarruq, no riba) and **routes the returns to your cause, in perpetuity.** Principal is never spent — it *works* forever.
4. **Dedicate it** — to your late father, your newborn, yourself. Name it. It carries that name forever.
5. **Watch it live** — a verified impact feed ("your endowment funded 3 months of Layla's tuition this quarter") and a perpetual projection ("your SAR 5,000 will give ≈ SAR 300 every year, forever — SAR 30,000 of giving in your first century, principal intact").

**The "only-now / only-here" element:** a *consumer-grade, AI-managed, fractional perpetual waqf with verified living impact* — impossible before 2026's cash-waqf + tokenized halal funds + digital endowment certificates + sovereign Arabic AI. Only an Islamic bank in the world's waqf heartland can credibly launch it.

## E. 72h build plan
- **Stack:** Arabic-first RTL app (React/Flutter) → service computing the perpetual projection + impact allocation → ALLaM (watsonx) for the Arabic guidance + impact narration → mock AWQAF endowment-fund return feed + OB sandbox (AIS funding/round-ups) + a Nafath mock for the endowment certificate → seeded verified-impact data.
- **Built for real:** the create-waqf flow, the **perpetual-projection model** (principal × halal return → forever-giving), the impact feed, the dedication/legacy view, the aggregate "national micro-waqf" pool stat. **Mocked:** the fund custody + Nafath certificate + live beneficiary verification (design the seam, seed the data).
- **The ONE feature that must work:** **create an Athar → see the perpetual projection + the dedication certificate**, in Arabic, in under a minute, offline-deterministic.

## F. Data story (shown live)
- A real AWQAF-style endowment **return rate** drives the projection; the screen shows the **forever curve** — principal flat, cumulative giving rising without end — contrasted against a normal donation (one bar, gone). Plus the impact-allocation breakdown and an aggregate "if 1% of Saudis endowed SAR 500" national-impact figure. Depth beyond one chart: projection × cause × time + the donation-vs-endowment contrast that *is* the pitch.

5. **2:40 — The vision:** "Ehsan let Saudis give SAR 9 billion — once. If 1% endow SAR 500, that's a **perpetual** national waqf that never stops giving. Alinma can launch the first consumer waqf on earth. **This is الباقيات الصالحات — made a product.**"

## H. Alinma-ship case (uncontested Shariah by design)
- **New category + sticky AUM:** endowed principal sits with Alinma **in perpetuity** — the stickiest deposit there is — while the bank earns management of the endowment funds. A new product line: **"Alinma Waqf."**
- **Shariah — a superpower, not a checkbox:** waqf is among the **most celebrated, uncontested** instruments in Islam; returns come from **equity-based halal funds** (no Tawarruq, no riba, no maysir/gharar). Only an Islamic bank can own it.
- **On-strategy + partnership:** aligns with **Vision 2030** and the **General Authority for Awqaf** (digital-certificate rails already live) → a partnership, a national-impact halo, and regional-first positioning. PDPL/consent/SAMA-clean (it's giving, not lending — minimal risk).
- **Vision ceiling:** the global waqf giant is asleep and tech-starved → **Alinma/Saudi could lead the region and the world** in democratized digital waqf.

## I. Differentiation (incl. "distinct from the kill-list")
- **vs Ehsan / donation apps:** they *spend* the gift once; Athar *endows* it to give forever. Category difference, not feature.
- **vs institutional waqf (AWQAF, Jood, Awqaf PAY):** those are registration/payments for big/institutional endowments; Athar is the **personal, micro, AI-managed, living-impact** consumer product none of them is.
- **vs the retired kill-list:** not Namaa (no reward/prize/maysir — this is *giving*, not saving-for-yourself); not Rushd (no compliance score); not Tadfuq/Haseen (no credit, no fraud). A genuinely new category.
