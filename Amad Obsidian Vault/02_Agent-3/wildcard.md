# 🃏 Wildcard (out-of-lane) — ميزان Mizan
> Out of Lane 3 (CX). This is **Track 3 — Financial Regulations (RegTech)**, bank-facing. *Mizan (ميزان)* = the scale/balance — the instrument of justice and exact measure.

## One-liner
**An AI copilot for the bank's Shariah board: it screens every new product and flags doubtful transactions against the bank's own fatwa canon, drafts the compliance memo with citations, and auto-generates the Shariah-audit trail — turning a slow, manual scholarly review into a same-day, fully-documented decision.**

## What it is
- A Shariah-board *back-office* tool (not customer-facing — that's [[concept-rushd-shariah-copilot|Rushd]]'s job). Mizan:
  1. **Screens** new product structures + transaction streams against a RAG corpus of the bank's prior rulings + AAOIFI standards → flags compliant / doubtful / non-compliant with the *closest precedent*.
  2. **Drafts** the Shariah-review memo (the structure, the precedent, the open questions) for a scholar to approve/edit — never auto-approves (human-in-the-loop).
  3. **Audits** — generates the immutable Shariah-compliance trail SAMA + the board need, and quantifies bank-level **purification** owed.
- **Only-now element:** ALLaM-grade Arabic legal reasoning + RAG over fiqh corpora makes scholarly precedent-search instant; impossible pre-2025.

## Track + requirements
- **Track 3 — Financial Regulations.** **/02 AI** (legal reasoning/RAG) + **/01 Data** (transaction screening + audit analytics).

## Why it could win
- **Uniquely Alinma:** every Islamic bank has a Shariah board that is a *bottleneck* on product velocity; Mizan is "ship Shariah-compliant products faster, provably." A bank exec feels the time-to-market pain immediately.
- **Defensible + governed:** human-in-the-loop, cited, audit-first — the responsible-AI story SAMA + Maqasid/XAI scholarship demand. [JCLI](https://jcli-bi.org/index.php/jcli/article/view/446)
- **Demoable in 72h:** screen a sample product → flag the doubtful clause → cite the precedent → draft the memo. Tight, visual, credible.

## Differentiation (incl. cross-agent)
- **vs Agent 2 [[concept-baseera|Baseera]] (AML + SAMA copilot):** different regulatory domain — Baseera = *financial-crime/prudential*; Mizan = *Shariah governance*. No overlap.
- **vs my own [[concept-rushd-shariah-copilot|Rushd]]:** Rushd is *customer-facing* conscience; Mizan is *bank-internal* board tooling. They'd actually compose (Mizan's canon could power Rushd's citations).
- **vs nothing else in the portfolio:** Shariah-governance RegTech is untouched by Agents 1/2/4.
