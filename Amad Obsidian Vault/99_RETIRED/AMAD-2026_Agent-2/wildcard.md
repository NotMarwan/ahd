# 🎴 Wildcard (out-of-lane) — درع · Dir' — Embedded Micro-Takaful for Gig Workers

> Out of my RegTech lane on purpose. Chosen to fill a domain **no other agent touches: Islamic cooperative insurance (takaful)**.
> **Pivot note:** my first wildcard was a digital *jam'iyyah* — killed because it collides with **Hakbah (1.3M users)** *and* **Agent 4's savings/gamification lane** (per [[saudi-fintech-terrain]]). Insurance is unclaimed by anyone in the portfolio.

**Track:** 2 — Customer Experience *(or 4 — Financial Education)* · **Req:** /02 AI + /04 Sustainable + /03 CX · Lane: 3/4-ish, **not** RegTech.

## One-liner
*A pay-as-you-earn, Shariah-compliant micro-**takaful** that lets a Saudi freelancer protect against the one risk that wrecks feast-or-famine income — a gap month or income shock — with AI that sizes coverage to their actual open-banking cashflow and auto-adjusts as gigs fluctuate.*

## Problem
- **150k+ registered freelancers** (مستقل) in KSA with **feast-or-famine income**, GOSI optional, thin safety nets — a Vision-2030 inclusion priority. A single bad month or an inability to work (illness/injury) has no buffer.
- Conventional income-protection insurance is (a) not designed for irregular gig income and (b) carries *gharar/riba* concerns that make it a poor fit for a Shariah-first customer. **Takaful** (cooperative risk-sharing from a *tabarru'* donation pool) is the Islamic-valid structure — and is **untouched by every other concept in this portfolio**.

## Solution
- AI reads the freelancer's **open-banking cashflow** (income volatility, gap-month frequency) and proposes a right-sized **micro-takaful** contribution — small, pay-as-you-earn (auto-skips lean months).
- Contributions flow into a **tabarru' (donation) risk pool**; payouts on a triggered event (income gap below threshold, certified inability to work) come from the pool — **no riba, no gharar-heavy conventional contract**; any surplus is returned/redistributed per takaful rules.
- GenAI handles Arabic onboarding, eligibility explanation, and claims triage; transparency dashboard shows the pool and the customer's standing.
