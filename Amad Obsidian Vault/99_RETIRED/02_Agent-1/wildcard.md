# 🃏 Agent 1 Wildcard — Rifqah (رفقة) · The Shariah-Smart Savings Circle, Reinvented
**Out-of-lane** (Tracks 6 Gamification + 4 Financial Education — Agent 4's turf; parked here for portfolio comparison). **Req:** /03 CX + /04 Sustainability.

## One-liner
**"Rifqah turns the traditional Saudi *jam'iya* (rotating savings circle) into a bank-backed, gamified, open-banking-verified savings product — social accountability that actually moves money, with an AI coach that nudges each member toward their goal."**

## Why it could win
- **Cultural fit:** the *jam'iya* is already how millions of Saudis save informally — formalizing it (vs inventing a new behavior) is a far easier adoption story than yet another budgeting app. Hakbah proved the demand; a *bank-backed, gamified, OB-verified* version with an AI coach is the next step.
- **Behavioral wow:** live progress rings, streaks, social circles, and milestone unlocks make the demo *feel* great — the emotional beat Lane-1 concepts lack.
- **Sustainability/inclusion narrative:** drives real, recurring savings behavior (Vision 2030 financial-inclusion), and extends Alinma's existing **gamification bet ("alinma Fantasy")**.
- **Shariah-native:** rotating savings with no interest is inherently compliant — a differentiator, not a constraint.

## Why it's NOT my champion
It optimizes **UX + innovation** but is **weaker on the data-depth + technical criteria** that Lane 1 nails, and the "Alinma ships it" P&L case is softer (savings engagement vs a SAR-0.5T lending book). Stronger as **Agent 4's** territory — flagged so the team can compare, and so Agent 4 can take or kill it without colliding with my lane.
