# 🔨 Raw attacks I ran on Kanaf & Sanad (then sealed → see Objection-Killer tables)

## كَنَف Kanaf (family-care treasury)
- **"It's just a joint account."** → Joint accounts have no fair-share, no per-contributor accountability, no dignity layer, no shame-free transparency. Sealed by the Fair-Share Engine + Dignity Layer.
- **"Families don't want the bank in their private money."** → It removes awkwardness, doesn't add it; the parent is shielded; sharing is opt-in per member. Reframed as *relief*.
- **"Someone refuses to pay — now what?"** → The product surfaces gently + lets others fairly cover; it never adjudicates a family fight. Honest boundary.
- **"Revenue?"** → Captures the entire family graph (3 gens) + recurring care deposits = the stickiest relationship in banking. Strong.
- **"Demo is just transfers."** → The fair-share rebalance + the parent's dignity view ("عائلتك معك") is the wow, and it's real computation.

## سَنَد Sanad (distributed social guarantee)
- **"This is the retired Tadfuq / lending in disguise."** → NO. The bank lends nothing; this is **kafāla (guarantee)**, money moves only on default, drawn from guarantors. Different mechanism + different (uncontested) Shariah structure. Sealed explicitly.
- **"Kafāla-for-a-fee is Shariah-contested."** → So the bank charges **no fee on the guarantee**; it monetizes via graduation + deposits. Debate sidestepped entirely.
- **"You'll pressure people into backing / over-expose guarantors."** → OB capacity-check + responsible sizing + **fair-draw (each pays only their portion, never joint-and-several)** + consent + caps + cooling-off.
- **"Cold-start / two-sided?"** → One-sided motivation: the requester has an acute need and drives it; backers are their loving circle (warm). Beats Ahd's symmetric cold ask.
- **"Enforceable?"** → Nafath-confirmed + Evidence Law 2022 admissible distributed kafāla.
