# Ahd — The 3-Minute Championship Demo (shot-by-shot)

> 180 seconds, live. Lead with the **relationship + the scripture (2:282)**, prove the **mechanisms** (not slides), land the **Muqassa wow**, close on **"Alinma = the trusted witness of money between people."** Every claim on screen is the **defensible** version (see `verification-ledger.md`). Deterministic, offline, RTL — no live external call on stage.
>
> Roles: **Speaker** (narrates) + **Driver** (runs the prototype). One screen. Seed bank pre-loaded (`AHD-001` Noura→Sara, `Circle-α` 9-IOU). Nafath/sarie/emdha mocked behind labelled `محاكاة` seams.

---

## BEAT 0 — The hook (0:00–0:20) · THE RELATIONSHIP + THE VERSE
**Screen:** black, then one line of Arabic fades in: **﴿يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا تَدَايَنتُم بِدَيْنٍ … فَاكْتُبُوهُ﴾ — البقرة ٢٨٢**.

**Speaker:**
> "Every Saudi has lent money to someone they love — and felt the same fear: *will this come back, and will it cost us the friendship?* Fourteen centuries ago, the **longest verse in the Quran** gave the answer: **اكتبوه — write it down.** Nobody does. Because asking the people you love to *sign* feels like an accusation."

**Beat.** Then:
> "**Ahd** makes writing it down an act of love — and makes Alinma the **just scribe** the verse names. Watch."

*(Why it lands: opens on the emotional + scriptural spine, names the exact cultural wound the product exists to heal. No product UI yet — earn the room first.)*

---

## BEAT 1 — Create + the lender signs FIRST (0:20–0:50) · THE REFRAME
**Screen:** Driver opens Ahd → "عهد جديد" → picks **قرض حسن** → Noura lends Sara **5,000 SAR**, 3 installments. ALLaM drafts plain-Arabic fair terms.

**Speaker:**
> "Noura lends Sara 5,000 riyals. Ahd drafts a fair, plain-Arabic covenant — **interest-free, no penalties**, exactly as qard hasan requires. And here is the move that changes everything: **Noura signs first.**"

**Screen:** Noura taps Nafath (`محاكاة`) → her signature lands. The invite that generates for Sara reads:
> **«نورة وفّت بوعدها وأعطتك ٥٠٠٠ ريال — قرضًا حسنًا بلا أي فائدة. أكّد استلامك.»**

**Speaker:**
> "Sara doesn't get a demand to *admit a debt*. She gets a **receipt of a kindness already given.** The lender gives first. That single inversion turns documentation from suspicion into a gift."

*(Mechanism shown: lender-signs-first ordering, qard_hasan kind, plain-Arabic ALLaM draft.)*

---

## BEAT 2 — The riba linter REFUSES a haram term (0:50–1:15) · THE SHARIAH PROOF
**Screen:** Driver says "what if someone tries to sneak in interest?" and toggles a **10% late penalty** into the terms. The badge flips **red**: **«غرامة تأخير (ربا الجاهلية) — مرفوض»**, and the **Nafath sign button disables.**

**Speaker:**
> "This is not a badge that always says 'halal.' It's a **live checker.** Add a late penalty — riba — and Ahd **refuses to let it be signed.** It blocks the haram term and disables the signature. It never issues a fatwa; it enforces the rules the Shariah board already set."

**Screen:** Driver removes the penalty → badge returns **green** «سليم» → sign button re-enables.

*(Mechanism shown: deterministic riba linter R2 + R1, the live toggle. This is the single most persuasive Shariah moment — a visible refusal.)*

---

## BEAT 3 — Dual-sign → the SEALED record (1:15–1:45) · THE WITNESS, PROVEN
**Screen:** Sara taps Nafath (`محاكاة`) → both signatures captured → the record SEALS. A certificate panel shows: **SHA-256 record root · Nafath identity refs (both) · trusted timestamp · chain link.** Label:
> **«سجلٌّ سليم التسلسل — كل تعديلٍ لاحقٍ يكسر السلسلة. مهيّأ للتقديم كدليلٍ إلكتروني وفق نظام الإثبات (م/٤٣)؛ القبول النهائي تقدير القاضي.»**

**Speaker:**
> "Now it's sealed. Not by a slogan — by a **SHA-256 hash chain, both parties' Nafath identities, and a trusted timestamp.** Edit one letter —" *(Driver clicks **تعديل** on the sealed terms)* "— and the chain **breaks**, visibly. We don't claim a court *must* accept it; we **engineer the integrity the law requires** and say plainly: the judge decides. The record names exactly what Alinma vouches for — **the signing event** — and what it refuses: that cash moved, that it's fair, that no one was pressured. **Alinma is the scribe, never the judge.**"

*(Mechanism shown: the SEAL + tamper-toggle + the scoped attestation boundary + the honest admissibility claim. Closes the K1/K2 kill-lines on stage.)*

---

## BEAT 4 — MUQASSA: the circle collapses (1:45–2:25) · THE WOW
**Screen:** Driver switches to **Circle-α** — 5 friends, **9 tangled IOUs** drawn as a messy graph. Numbers everywhere.

**Speaker:**
> "Now the real magic — for the group chat that always ends in 'who owes who?' Five friends, **nine tangled debts.**"

**Screen:** Driver taps **«قاصّوا الدائرة»**. The graph collapses to **2 arrows**. Each member gets a one-tap consent card: **«تتحول التزاماتك إلى تحويلٍ واحد لـ‹خالد›. توافق؟»** All approve → **MUQASSA** event chains onto each ahd.

**Speaker:**
> "**Nine obligations become two transfers.** Each person pays or receives — at most one fewer transfer than there are people, provably. And we don't silently reassign anyone's debt: **every member consents** to their new leg. It's classical **مقاصّة** — a witnessed agreement-of-agreements — settled over sarie in one tap."

*(Mechanism shown: the consented netting, ≤ P−1 claim — NOT "minimum," NOT "each once" — per-party consent. This is the Data centerpiece: real computation, not a chart.)*

## BEAT 5 — ذِمّة محفوظة + the close (2:25–3:00) · THE SOUL
**Screen:** back to `AHD-001`. Driver fires the final auto-settle (`محاكاة` sarie) → schedule clears to **exactly** 5,000 → both phones show, at the same instant:
> **«ذمّةٌ محفوظة. وفّيتما بعهدكما — وبقيت المودّة.»**

**Speaker (slow):**
> "The loan is repaid — to the halala. And both of them see the same words: **the obligation is honoured, and the affection remained.** That is the dignity the verse promised."

**Screen:** logo + one line: **عهد — الشاهد الأمين على المال بين الناس.**

**Speaker (close):**
> "Ahd is the first **bank-witnessed rail for money between people.** The bank lends nothing — it **writes, witnesses, and settles**, exactly as the Quran's longest verse commands. It needs no finance licence, it can't charge riba by construction, and it spreads because the lender gives first. **Only an Islamic bank can be the trusted witness of a Saudi's word — and Alinma can be it first.**"

## On-screen claim discipline (the non-negotiables)
- Admissibility → "**مهيّأ/مصمّمة** لتستوفي شروط القبول؛ القبول النهائي تقدير القاضي."
- Muqassa → "**كل طرف يدفع أو يقبض بأقل تحويلات** (≤ عدد الأطراف − ١)"; never "أقل عدد".
- Fee → if shown: "**رسم خدمة ثابت** (توثيق/وكالة) — لا علاقة له بمبلغ القرض"; default: **مجاني للمستخدم.**
- Nafath → labelled **محاكاة**; the binding signature is "Nafath + **مزوّد توقيع مرخّص (emdha)**."
