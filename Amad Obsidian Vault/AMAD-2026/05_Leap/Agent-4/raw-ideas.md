# Phase 2 — Diverge. Invent mechanisms, not clones. Discard the obvious first.

Gut-check 1–5: **S** surprise · **E** emotion · **F** 72h-feasibility. Shariah-clean by design (no Tawarruq/maysir/gharar).

## 🗑️ The safe-3 (discarded as the price of entry — these are what everyone proposes)
- ~~Digital qard-hassan **lending circle** (P2P interest-free)~~ → too close to Hakbah/jam'iyah; "lending platform" is a known shape, not a leap.
- ~~**Waqf crowdfunding** platform~~ → already exists in many forms; not new.
- ~~**Sadaqah round-up** donation app~~ → done globally; Ehsan owns national charity. No surprise.

## Clusters
- **A · Dyadic trust/record** (1, 5, 6, 7, 9, 12, 17, 19) → **عهد Ahd** absorbs these. #6 Muqassa (debt-netting) and #17 (forgiveness) become *killer features* of Ahd — netting adds a real graph-algorithm/data story.
- **B · Conditional release** (2, 16) → **أمانة Amana** (escrow / yadan-bi-yad).
- **C · Collective** (3, 11, 13) → **تكاتف Takatuf** (ta'awun group power).
- **D · Faith-positive giving** (4, 8, 20) → **مُعلّق Mu'allaq** wildcard (Vector-3-adjacent on purpose).
- **E · Discarded:** 10 (kafala connotation + lending-adjacent), 14 (faraid = A3 + death-verification infeasible), 15/18 (abstract / shaming risk), 22 (mere feature), 21 (A2 pilgrim territory).

## Advancing to Phase 3 (the 3 most *surprising-yet-buildable*, not the 3 safest)
1. **[[concept-ahd|عهد Ahd]]** — champion candidate: the witnessed-money primitive (Ayat al-Dayn). Highest S+E, uncontested, viral, Alinma-ownable.
2. **[[concept-amana|أمانة Amana]]** — conditional trust-money / escrow; different wedge (commerce/deals vs personal promises).
3. **[[concept-takatuf|تكاتف Takatuf]]** — collective economic action; different bet (many-to-one vs dyadic).

Wildcard (cross-lane → Vector 3): **[[wildcard|مُعلّق Mu'allaq]]** — suspended giving.
