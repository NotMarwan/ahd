# 🤲 كَرامة · Karama — "Give the worker his wage before his sweat dries."

> The Prophet ﷺ: *"أعطوا الأجير أجره قبل أن يجفّ عرقه."* The most invisible Saudis of all are the millions of **domestic workers** — housemaids, drivers, nannies — paid in cash, unbanked, financially erased, and exposed to withheld wages. No bank builds for them. Karama gives them a *dignified financial identity*.

**Segment (Invisible Saudis):** domestic workers (the deepest financial invisibility in the Kingdom).
**Track:** 2 — Customer Experience · **Requirements:** /03 CX + /04 Sustainability + /01 Data
**Score:** **80 / 100**

## A. One-liner
*A worker-first account that turns an invisible, cash-paid domestic worker into a seen, banked, protected person: their wage is paid digitally and verifiably on time, it cannot be quietly withheld, and they control their own saving and sending, building the first financial record of their lives.*

## B. Problem
Millions of domestic workers live and work in Saudi homes, **paid in cash, with no bank account, no financial record, and no recourse** when wages are delayed or withheld. They are the most financially invisible people in the Kingdom, despite a clear Islamic command to pay a worker promptly and fully. Wage protection exists for corporate payroll (WPS/Mudad) but **stops at the household door**; the domestic worker remains outside the system, exploited and undignified.

## C. Solution
A worker-controlled account opened on **iqama + Nafath**, designed for low-literacy use (pairs naturally with [[concept-sawt|Sawt]] voice-first):
1. **Verified wage in.** The employer pays the monthly wage digitally (via the **Musaned** domestic-labor platform rail) → the worker is paid **on time, in full, provably** — withholding becomes visible, not silent.
2. **The worker controls it.** They save toward a goal, send a portion home, and spend — *they* hold the account, not the employer.
3. **A financial identity is born.** Each on-time wage builds the worker's first verifiable financial record — the foundation of dignity and future inclusion.
- **Only-now/only-here:** Nafath digital ID + instant rails + Musaned reforms make a *worker-held, verified-wage* account feasible for the first time; the household-labor gap is a uniquely-KSA structural problem to solve first.

## D. Mapping
Track 2 (CX). /03 CX (a humane, dignified experience for the excluded) + /04 Sustainability (long-term inclusion of an entire workforce) + /01 Data (the wage-verification + emerging financial record).

## E. 72h build plan
**Built:** worker onboarding (iqama/Nafath, voice-assisted), the verified-wage-in event (employer pays → worker confirmed paid on time), worker-controlled save + send, the "your record" identity view, an employer compliance view (paid/late status).
**Mocked:** real Musaned/MHRSD integration, live cross-border remittance settlement, KYC edge cases. Synthetic worker+employer personas.
**Must-work:** employer pays wage → worker receives it in *their own* controlled account → withholding/late-payment is flagged → worker sends a portion home + saves toward a goal.

## F. Data story
A wage-timeliness + financial-identity view: on-time vs delayed payments across months; the worker's growing record; an aggregate "X% of wages now paid on time vs the cash baseline." Real, dignifying analytics on a population that had *zero* data before.

## G. Demo script (3 min)
Open on the hadith and the truth: "Millions keep our homes running. Not one has a bank account or proof they were paid." A worker onboards by voice. The employer pays the wage digitally; the worker — not the employer — receives and controls it; a withheld-wage attempt is flagged. She sends part home and starts her first-ever savings goal. Close: "We made the most invisible Saudis visible, paid, and protected. That is what an Islamic bank is *for*."

## H. Alinma-ship case (Shariah-clean & positive)
A vast untapped deposit + remittance base; a flagship **Vision-2030 inclusion + labor-reform** story; profound CSR + brand. **Shariah-clean and positive:** it is pure accounts/payments (no riba, no contract issue) and it *enforces a core Islamic command* — paying the worker promptly and fully (ḥaqq al-ʿāmil). Faith as justice. PDPL/Nafath consent; rides Musaned/sarie rails; SAMA-aligned.

## I. Differentiation
vs **Mudad/WPS** (employer-side corporate payroll compliance — stops at households; not worker-held) and vs **remittance apps** (commodity money-movement, no identity, no wage protection, no dignity). Karama is **worker-first identity + verified wage + control**. Distinct from the kill-list and from the other agents' faith-mechanic concepts (it's a justice/inclusion segment play). **Swapped in for my retired Wasiyyah finalist (ceded to Agent 3).**

## J. Risk register
1. **Two-sided adoption (needs the employer to pay in).** → Lead with the employer's compliance/peace-of-mind + the Musaned mandate as the wedge; the worker side is the heart.
2. **Regulatory integration (Musaned/MHRSD).** → Frame as a partnership/rail; mock for the demo; cite the existing WPS precedent.
3. **"Is this a bank product or charity?"** → It's a real deposit + payments + remittance book on a huge population; the dignity is the differentiator, not the P&L gap.
