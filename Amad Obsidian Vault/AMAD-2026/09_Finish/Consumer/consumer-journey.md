# 🫶 Ahd — Consumer Soul · The Lived Journey

> **Agent 3 · Consumer Soul.** I stopped being an architect and became three ordinary Saudis. I walked the **real** prototype (`project/ahd-demo/index.html`) screen by screen, in first person, feeling by feeling — not validating the design, hunting for where it feels **cold, confusing, or transactional**, so I could mark where it must turn **warm**. Findings feed Agent 2 (fix-up) and Agent 4 (integrator). On-spine throughout (bank = scribe, lends nothing); Shariah-clean (warmth, never manipulation or shame).
>
> **Legend:** 🟢 warm/felt-held · 🟡 confusing/hesitation · 🔴 cold/transactional · 🩶 *missing moment* (the feeling has no screen at all).

---

## The structural truth I hit in the first 60 seconds

The prototype is a **single-device "god view."** On the witnessing screen, **both** نورة (lender) and سارة (borrower) sit as two cards and get tapped on the *same* screen. That's fine for a 3-minute judge demo — but it means **the borrower never has a moment of their own.** The single most important emotional event in the whole product — *a person receiving the ask* — **does not exist as a screen.** The growth layer already proved this is the make-or-break beat (the "gift-receipt, not an accusation" inversion, [[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.3). The prototype asserts it in prose and shows none of it.

And the emotional shape of the journey is **cold → cold → cold → warm**: a form, a courtroom, an auto-payment, then *one* beautiful line at the very end («ذمّة محفوظة»). The warmth is real but rationed to the finale. A user who's nervous about *asking their mother to sign an IOU* has already felt cold three times before the app ever reassures them.

## Persona A — منى، ٢٣، طالبة، تقاسم سكن
*Just paid the full flat rent (3,200 ر.س); her flatmate رهف owes her half. Low stakes, high frequency, lives with the person — the awkwardness is daily.*

| Screen (real) | منى's inner voice | Read |
|---|---|---|
| **0 · The problem** «أكثر تعاملٍ ماليٍّ شيوعًا… بلا منتج» + الآية | "Wallah this is exactly me and رهف. And the آية… I never thought of it like that — توثيق is *sunnah*, not me being cheap." | 🟢 The ayah is the best hook in the app. It reframes before she even starts. |
| **0 → button** «لنبدأ — قرضٌ بين صديقتين» | "Wait — قرض? It's not a loan, رهف just owes me her rent share. Where's *تقاسم* (splitting)?" | 🟡 The demo hard-codes a 5,000 *qard* between two women; منى's actual case (a shared cost) isn't the entry. The product *has* "shared cost" in the model (contracts C2) but the demo only tells the loan story. |
| **1 · Create** form: نورة → سارة 5,000, علّام types terms, ✓ خالٍ من الربا | "It's writing the terms itself, nice. But it feels like… filing a form at a government office. Where's *me*? I'm doing رهف a favour and it feels like paperwork." | 🔴 The act of helping a friend is rendered as a data-entry form. No acknowledgement that this is a *kindness*. The riba badges reassure a *judge*, not منى. |
| **2 · Witness** «يؤكّد الطرفان هويّتهما — فيُختَم العهد كدليلٍ لا يقبل العبث» + tamper toggle | "«دليلٌ لا يقبل العبث»… «جرّب العبث بالمبلغ»… this feels like we're getting ready for *court*. Me and رهف? I just want her half of the rent." | 🔴 The witnessing is framed as **evidence against tampering** — a litigation frame. For two flatmates it whispers *distrust*, the exact wound the product claims to heal. (Brilliant for judges; cold for منى.) |
| **3 · Settle** auto-installments → «ذمّة محفوظة · صداقةٌ سالمة» | "Okay *this* is nice — «صداقةٌ سالمة، وعهدٌ وُفِّي به». That's the feeling I wanted." | 🟢 The finale lands. But it's the first warm thing in ~2 minutes. |
| **4 · Muqassa** | "Oh this is clever — but it's a different story (five friends). For me and رهف it's overkill." | 🟢/🟡 Impressive, but not منى's lane. |

**منى's verdict:** *"It works, but it treats my favour like a contract. I'd use it because the ayah convinced me توثيق is right — but it never once made me feel good about helping رهف."* Her recurring case (monthly rent) — the retention gold from [[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.4 — **isn't even visible** in the lived flow.

## Persona B — أم خالد (فاطمة)، ٥٢، تُقرض ابنها · and خالد، ٢٦، الابن
*The make-or-break persona.* A **mother** lends her son خالد 5,000 for a car repair. Highest emotional stakes in the whole product: asking your *son* to e-sign an IOU is the most distrust-laden act imaginable — **and** فاطمة is an older, less app-fluent user. I walk **both** sides, because the borrower's side is the missing screen.

### فاطمة (the mother, lending)
| Screen | Her inner voice | Read |
|---|---|---|
| **0 · Problem** | "كله صحيح، بس… أوثّق على *ابني*؟ يمكن يزعل، يحس إني ما أثق فيه." | 🟡 The fear arrives *immediately* and the app doesn't catch it. The ayah helps a little, but she needs reassurance that this won't hurt خالد's feelings. |
| **1 · Create** the typing terms, legal-ish Arabic | "الخط صغير والكلام رسمي… «يُقِرّ الطرفان»… أنا بس أبي أساعد ولدي، ليش يصير الكلام كذا محكمة؟" | 🔴🟡 The auto-drafted terms read like a legal deed («يُقِرّ الطرفان… دون أيّ زيادة أو فائدة»). For a 52-year-old helping her son, the register is cold and a little intimidating. No "read it to me in simple words." |
| **2 · Witness** | "لازم خالد «يأكّد ببصمة نفاذ»… ويختم «دليل لا يقبل العبث»… يا ساتر، كأنه قضية." | 🔴 The litigation frame is *most* damaging here. A mother does not want a tamper-proof case file against her son. |
| **3 · Settle / ذمّة محفوظة** | "حلو إنه يسوّي بدون ما أطالبه… بس وش لو ما قدر يدفع شهر؟ بيتضايق، وأنا ما أبي أحرجه." | 🩶 Her deepest worry — *what if my son struggles to pay?* — has **no answer in the app.** The settle screen pays perfectly every time. The most "held" she could feel (knowing the app protects خالد if he stumbles, ﴿وإن كان ذو عسرة فنظرة إلى ميسرة﴾) is **absent.** |

### خالد (the son, receiving) 🩶 **— THIS ENTIRE EXPERIENCE IS MISSING**
In the prototype خالد is a card that gets tapped. In real life he receives *something* — and **nothing in the app is designed for that moment.** What he'd feel with the current framing:
- A link: *"confirm you owe your mother 5,000, signed evidence, tamper-proof."* → 🔴 "أمي ما تثق فيني؟ توثّق عليّ زي البنك؟" — **the accusation feeling.** This is exactly the cultural wound, and the product currently *delivers* it instead of disarming it.
- What he *should* receive (per [[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.3, but it has no screen): *"أمك وفت بوعدها وأعطتك ٥٠٠٠ — قرضًا حسنًا، بلا فائدة ولا غرامة. أكّد استلامك، وسدّد متى ما تيسّر."* → 🟢 a **gift-receipt**: his mother's kindness first, his protection (no riba, ease on hardship) explicit, the obligation last and soft.

**This is the single highest-value gap in the product.** The borrower's receiving-moment is both the most emotionally dangerous beat *and* completely unbuilt.

## Persona C — سلطان، ٢٧، رحلة مع الشباب
*Went on a trip with 4 friends; he fronted the chalet, خالد covered fuel, each owes bits to each other — a tangled web. He both owes and is owed.* Tests the Muqassa payoff and the debtor-side feeling.

| Screen | His inner voice | Read |
|---|---|---|
| **0–3** (the loan story) | "هذا مو موقفي بالضبط — أنا مو مقرض واحد، إحنا خمسة وكلٌّ على كلّ." | 🟡 The linear loan story doesn't match the *circle* case until screen 4. |
| **4 · Muqassa** graph + «شغّل المقاصّة» → 9⟶2 + conservation table | "وااو. تسعة تحويلات صارت تحويلين؟ يعني ما بقى أحد يطالب أحد؟ هذا اللي كنت أتجنّبه — الإحراج إني أطلب فلوسي من ربعي." | 🟢 The *idea* is a genuine "oh!" — **but the feeling the table delivers is "math," not "relief."** "برهان الحفظ… لا مال يُخلق ولا يضيع" reassures an auditor. What سلطان feels is *"finally nobody has to chase anybody"* — and the app never says that. |
| **4 · reputation rings** «سجلّ الوفاء» % per friend | "ليش فيه نسب فوق روس ربعي؟ ٨٧٪… ٩٢٪… كأنه تقييم. أحس إنه يحكم على أصحابي." | 🟡🔴 The kept-ratio rings, shown as **percentages over friends' heads**, read like a credit-score/rating of his friends — the one thing the product swears it isn't. Technically "social," emotionally "judgmental." |

**سلطان's verdict:** *"The netting is magic, but it shows me a spreadsheet. The magic is that the awkwardness disappears — say *that*."*

## 🗺️ The cold→warm friction map (the deliverable for Agent 2 & 4)

| # | Moment (real screen) | What it feels like now | What turns it warm | Sev |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **C1** | The whole borrower side | 🩶 **No screen** — borrower is a tapped card | A dedicated **gift-receipt invite screen** (deed first, protection explicit, obligation last) | **Fatal** (it's the make-or-break beat) |
| **C2** | "What if I can't pay?" | 🩶 No screen — settle always pays perfectly | A **يُسر / ﴿نظرة إلى ميسرة﴾ safety beat**: reassurance at signing + an "أحتاج وقت" reschedule-together flow, never a penalty | **Fatal** (deepest "held" feeling) |
| **C3** | Screen 2 witnessing | 🔴 "دليلٌ لا يقبل العبث / جرّب العبث" = a courtroom against your friend | Lead with **إشهاد as honouring** ("نُوثّق كلمتكما لتبقى"); keep the tamper-proof as a *"كيف نحفظها؟"* reveal behind a tap | Serious |
| **C4** | Screen 1 create | 🔴 A favour rendered as a government form | A **giving-intention beat** ("معروفك يُحفظ"; *every qard is sadaqah*) before the form | Serious |
| **C5** | Screen 3 finale only | 🟢 but rationed to the very end | Thread micro-warmth throughout (receipt confirmations, a gentle dua) so warmth isn't saved for minute 3 | Serious |
| **C6** | "Will it nag my mother?" | 🩶 "بلا تذكير محرج" asserted, never shown | **Show the reminder**: a rail-sent, ease-toned preview ("متى ما تيسّر 🌿") so the user trusts it won't embarrass them | Serious |
| **C7** | Muqassa table | 🔴 "برهان الحفظ" = math/audit | Add the **human line of relief**: "ما بقى أحد يطالب أحد" before the proof | Cosmetic-but-high-leverage |
| **C8** | Reputation rings as % | 🟡🔴 Reads like rating your friends | Reframe as a **private leaf of وفاء** (✓/🌿), drop the % over friends' heads; keep it self-facing | Serious (it risks the "not a credit score" promise *emotionally*) |
| **C9** | Older lender (فاطمة) | 🔴 Small legal Arabic, no assist | A **"بالكلام البسيط / اسمعيها" plain-language + read-aloud** option on the terms | Serious (inclusion = dignity) |
| **C10** | ذمّة محفوظة | 🟢 good, but solo & one-shot | Make it **shared** (both feel it) + a **dua** + a **kept-word stamp** ("عهدك الـ١٣ الذي وُفِّي به") | High-leverage delight |

**The single most important finding:** the product's emotional thesis — *signing is an act of care, not suspicion* — lives entirely in the **borrower's receiving moment and the "what if I stumble" moment**, and **both are absent from the prototype.** Everything else is polish; these two are the soul. If we build only two things this round, build C1 and C2.
