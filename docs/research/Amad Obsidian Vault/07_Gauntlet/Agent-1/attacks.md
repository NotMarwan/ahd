# 🗡️ Self-attacks I ran on my own two — before sealing
> The Executioner's pass on Nisab & Birr. Each hole found → how the design was changed to close it (now reflected in the Objection-Killer tables).

## نِصاب Nisab
| Hole the Executioner found | What I changed (seal) |
|---|---|
| "It's a zakat calculator — those are everywhere; pattern-match death." | Re-centred the category on **continuous + OB-connected + Hijri-hawl + auto-discharge**. Killed any framing as a "calculator"; the hero is the engine *acting on the right day*, not a form. |
| "AI computing zakat = fatwa risk (the operator's ban)." | Made the engine apply a **board-validated Hanbali ruleset that shows its working**; edge cases **escalate to a human/board**; the AI never rules. Added this as Shariah row + §12. |
| "Overclaims it sees all your wealth." | Added explicit **manual-add + honest coverage** ("what we see + what you add"), never omniscience. (Risk #2.) |
| "Where's the live wow? A number isn't a wow." | Engineered the **calculator-vs-Nisab error contrast** ("you'd have overpaid SAR 900") — a concrete, surprising, screenshot-able beat. |
| "Al Rajhi copies it." | Moat = **continuous aggregation + correctness + discharge rails + Hijri engine + Islamic-bank trust**, not a form (Differentiation row). |
| Frequency (Agent 3's axis): "annual = low retention." | The product is **always-on** (continuous tracking, monthly hawl timers) — not an annual event. Explicitly an engine, not a once-a-year tool. |

**Verdict:** all 10 vectors sealed. Remaining honest disclosure: live OB + sarie discharge are mocked behind seams (every concept this round is); the ruleset is a curated Hanbali subset.

## بِرّ Birr
| Hole the Executioner found | What I changed (seal) |
|---|---|
| "It's Athar's dedication feature renamed." | Sharpened the **spine**: Birr *settles a deceased parent's unfinished obligations* + continues giving — a *relationship*, not a forward endowment. Made the differentiation row explicit. |
| "It infers obligations like Dhimmah → false positives + false spiritual assurance." | Designed it to work from **heir-authorized estate data + explicit declaration**, never inference; and it **completes real acts** rather than declaring an account "clear." This is the exact hole I killed Dhimmah on — sealed in my own. |
| "Morbid / grief-exploitation." | Tone re-engineered to **reverent + consoling + opt-in, customer-initiated**; "their record" is comfort. Tone made a first-class design constraint. |
| "Death is a rare event → no retention." | Reframed: **every Muslim loses parents (universal)** + birr **continues for years** (recurring sadaqah) → ongoing engagement, sealing Agent 3's frequency axis. |
| "Acting financially for the dead — is it even valid?" | Grounded every act in **cited, consensus fiqh** (debts/zakat/Hajj-badal/sadaqah on behalf); board-validated; AI never rules. |
| "Heir could act on an estate they're not entitled to." | **Nafath/Absher heir-verification** + inheritance-law scoping as a gate. (Risk #1.) |

**Verdict:** all 10 vectors sealed. Birr deliberately **takes Dhimmah's emotional power and removes its ethical hole** (no false assurance; real completion).

## Cross-check: are my two distinct (not two variants)?
- **Nisab** = computational fulfillment of *your own* living obligation (zakat), always-on, data-deep.
- **Birr** = relational devotion to *the deceased*, emotional, recurring.
- Different spines, different emotions, different tracks (Open Banking vs CX). They **compose** (Birr reuses Nisab's zakat engine) without being the same idea. ✅
