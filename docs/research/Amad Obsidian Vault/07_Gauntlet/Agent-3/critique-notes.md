# 🧭 Agent 3 — critique contribution + gap map
My full teardown of the 4 champions lives in the shared **[[teardown]]** (Agent-1 seeded all four thoroughly; I appended the two axes the data-focused teardown under-weighted).

## My two added attack axes (the holes I built into)
1. **FREQUENCY / RETENTION** — the portfolio's hidden shared weakness. Every champion is low-frequency or trust-gated: Athar (one-time endow), Dhimmah (once-in-a-lifetime Hajj), Wakeel (autonomy users disable), Ahd (only when lending a friend — and asking them to sign **offends** the relationship). **None is a daily, durable bank relationship.**
2. **SEGMENT** — all four serve the **practising mainstream Saudi**. The *truly invisible* (foreign guests, the disabled, the aging) are unserved by the whole board.

## Gap map → my two fortresses
Both are **everyday, data-rich relationships** on **uncopyable real-world infrastructure** — sealing the frequency + data holes at once, in two distinct directions:
- **[[concept-dayf|ضيف Dayf]]** — the *transient* invisible (foreign pilgrim guests): real FX/ritual/fraud data, daily during the trip, a Vision-2030 funnel that re-fills every season. *Goes beyond Dhimmah (operations+protection, not the Saudi's conscience).*
- **[[concept-waqar|وقار Waqar]]** — the *lifelong* invisible (the aging): daily use, real behavioral data, multi-generation lock-in. *Distinct from A1's Birr (deceased) and A2's Sawt (voice-only).*
