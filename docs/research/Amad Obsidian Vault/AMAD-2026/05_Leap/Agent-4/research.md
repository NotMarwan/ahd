# Phase 1 Recon — Vector 4: A New Primitive

Mandate: invent a genuinely new, Shariah-clean financial *mechanism* (not a clone) — a new way money moves between people, a non-lending trust primitive, a collective mechanic, or an OB capability nobody has used OB for. Surprise mandatory. 8 live searches. Sources at bottom.

## The unserved human truth (the emotional core)
Money **between people** is the most common, most fraught, least-served financial surface — and it's a relationship-killer:
- **~31%** are owed money by a loved one; **1 in 6** say money has *ruined* a relationship; among those who lent/borrowed, **75%** say they're no longer as close.
- **49.9% never put the loan in writing**; **49.5%** had no repayment timeline; **54.5%** had to ask more than once; **30% of borrowers never repaid.**
- The fix is **literally scripture:** the Quran's longest verse, **Ayat al-Dayn (2:282)**, commands believers to **write down debts and have them witnessed.** A billion Muslims are told to do this; **no one has built the product for it.**

→ The leap: a primitive that makes money between people **clear, fair, witnessed, and kept** — turning the most common cause of broken trust into a moment of preserved trust. *Faith is the feature, not a constraint.*

## What exists (white space confirmed)
| Adjacent thing | What it does | The gap it leaves |
|---|---|---|
| **Splitwise / Tricount** (used in KSA) | *Tracks* shared expenses | No Arabic, no settlement, no witnessing, no enforcement — just a ledger |
| **sarie** (SAMA instant rails) | Moves money P2P instantly (proxy by mobile/ID; ≤20k/txn) | Raw rails — irreversible, unconditional, no record of *why/terms* |
| **Najiz / MoJ e-documentation** | Notarizes POAs, marriage, property; **Evidence Law 2022** makes e-records + Nafath e-signatures legally admissible | Does **not** touch ordinary interpersonal *money* agreements (loans, IOUs, promises) |
| **Qard-hassan fintech (KSA, emerging)** | Institution→person microloans, EWA, crowdfunding | No **peer-to-peer** "record + witness + auto-settle the loan between two friends" |
| **Haraj (C2C marketplace)** | Huge informal trade | **No escrow / dispute resolution** — "pay deposit, seller vanishes" scams are rife |
| **Takadao** | P2P mutual takaful (insurance) | Collective-risk only; not a general money-trust primitive |

**Nobody owns the "money between people" trust layer.** Banks treat it as out-of-scope; marketplaces punt it; Splitwise just counts.

## The 2026 capability that makes the leap possible NOW
- **Nafath** biometric MFA digital identity → two ordinary people can **mutually, bindingly consent** to terms in seconds.
- **Evidence Law 2022** → electronic records + Nafath-tied signatures are **admissible legal evidence** (non-repudiation). A bank-witnessed money-agreement now carries real weight.
- **sarie** → **automatic settlement** of the agreed installments/conditions (proxy addressing, 24/7).
- **ALLaM / watsonx (Alinma stack)** → drafts **fair, plain-Arabic terms** and keeps them **qard-hassan-clean** (flags any drift toward riba/penalty).

## Shariah = superpower (uncontested by design)
- **Ayat al-Dayn (2:282)** mandates recording + witnessing debts → the product *is* the sunnah of documentation.
- **Qard hassan** (benevolent, interest-free loan) is *encouraged*, the Shariah-**positive** opposite of the retired Tawarruq. The bank charges **no interest, no penalty** — at most a flat **wakala** service fee (or free as CX).
- **Amana / wakala** (trust + agency): the bank as a neutral **trustee/witness** is theologically ideal — and **only an Islamic bank would frame it this way.**
- The escrow sibling restores **"yadan bi-yad"** (hand-to-hand simultaneous exchange) for remote deals → it *removes* gharar (the opposite of the contested structures we're avoiding).

## Buildable in 72h (the demo is real)
- Two Nafath-mock identities → create an agreement → ALLaM drafts Arabic terms → both confirm → tamper-evident record → auto-reminders → **auto-settle via (mock) sarie** → "dhimmah cleared." All demoable on synthetic data + the Alinma OB sandbox; settlement stubbed behind a clean seam.

## Alinma-ship logic
- **Float / deposits** from amana-held funds (escrow sibling) = cheap funding.
- **Acquisition + engagement:** *every* Saudi has money owed to/from someone — a universal, viral, daily-use hook (each agreement pulls in a second user).
- **Brand moat:** Alinma becomes "the trusted witness of money between people" — a category only an Islamic bank can credibly own.
- **Non-credit trust capital:** a history of kept agreements = a social reputation signal (NOT a credit score — deliberately not underwriting, to stay off the retired Thiqa/Tadfuq turf).

## Three primitives this recon surfaces (→ finalists)
1. **عهد Ahd — Witnessed Money:** record + witness + auto-settle interpersonal money (qard hassan, IOUs, promises). *Champion candidate.*
2. **أمانة Amana — Conditional "trust money":** money held in trust, auto-released on verified proof (C2C deals/Haraj, freelance milestones, deposits) — digital "yadan bi-yad."
3. **تكاتف Takatuf — Collective economic action:** transparent, Shariah-clean pooling for group power (occasion bulk-buys, community funds, shared goals) — ta'awun as a primitive.
