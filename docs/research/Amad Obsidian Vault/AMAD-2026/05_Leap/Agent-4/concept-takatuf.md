# تكاتف · Takatuf — "Alone you're a customer. Together you're a force."
**Track:** 2 Customer Experience (alt: 6 Gamification for the collective mechanic) · **Requirements:** /03 CX + /01 Data + /04 Sustainability
**Score: 80 / 100**

## A. One-liner
*Takatuf turns a group of ordinary people into collective economic power — pool money transparently and Shariah-cleanly to unlock what none could alone: a bulk Qurban at wholesale, a neighborhood emergency fund, a family goal — governed by rules everyone can see, settled by the bank.*

## B. Problem
Individuals are price-takers; the informal "we'll chip in" happens constantly (Qurban shares, group gifts, building maintenance, family goals, mutual aid) but runs on a WhatsApp thread and one person's overloaded bank account — **no transparency, no governance, no trust, no power.** Saudi/Islamic culture is built on **ta'awun** ("cooperate in righteousness," Quran 5:2) and the prophetic image of the believers as **one body / one hand** — yet there's no primitive that gives a group financial agency. Group-buying platforms exist abroad (Pinduoduo) but as commerce funnels, not as trusted, value-driven, Shariah-clean collective finance.

## C. Solution
A new primitive: **a governed collective pool with transparent rules.** A convener starts a Takatuf for a purpose; members join and contribute (auto via sarie); everyone sees the live ledger; **rules are explicit and enforced** (target, deadline, refund-if-unmet, payout conditions, who can approve). Flavors:
- **Collective buy:** reach a quorum → unlock a wholesale price (Qurban, Ramadan staples, bulk goods).
- **Community fund:** a neighborhood/profession pool that pays a member in crisis by transparent vote/rule (mutual aid, *not* an insurance company).
- **Shared goal:** a family/majlis fund with contribution roles toward one aim.
**Only-now/only-here:** sarie auto-contribution + OB visibility + Nafath group identity + ALLaM rule-drafting — turned onto culturally-loaded Saudi occasions.

## D. Mapping
Track 2 CX (alt 6 Gamification — the collective progress mechanic). Requirements **/03** (a delightful group experience) + **/01** (transparent ledger + quorum/price analytics) + **/04** (community resilience = sustainability; ta'awun).

## E. 72h build plan
- **Built:** create-a-Takatuf flow + rule engine (target/deadline/refund/payout), join + auto-contribute (mock sarie), the **live transparent ledger**, quorum→price-unlock logic, payout/refund execution, an occasion template (Qurban).
- **Mocked:** merchant wholesale integration, Nafath, sarie.
- **Must-work:** members contribute → quorum hit → wholesale price unlocks → transparent payout; and the refund-if-unmet path.

## F. Data story
Live: the **collective ledger + quorum thermometer**, the **price-curve** (unit price drops as members join — real collective-bargaining math), and a community-resilience metric. Genuine analytics, shown live.

## G. Demo script (3 min)
1. "One family pays retail for Qurban. Fifty families pay wholesale — if someone could organize the trust. We do."
2. Convener starts an Eid Qurban Takatuf; families join; the **price-per-share drops live** as the quorum fills.
3. Quorum hit → price locks at wholesale → transparent payout to the verified vendor; everyone sees every riyal.
4. Flip to a **neighborhood emergency fund** paying a member in crisis by transparent rule. (Emotional beat — mutual aid.)
5. Close: "Ta'awun, with rails. Alinma powers Saudi communities to act as one — a category only a values-led bank would build."

## H. Bank-ship case
- **Deposits** (pooled balances) + **merchant/commerce fees** + mass **acquisition** (one convener pulls in dozens) + a deeply **on-brand** community/Vision-2030 story.
- **Shariah:** ta'awun (Quranic), transparent, no riba/maysir/gharar; mutual-aid pools are cooperative (not commercial insurance). **PDPL/SAMA** clean.

## I. Differentiation
- vs **Hakbah/jam'iyah:** that's rotating *individual savings*; Takatuf is *collective purpose + power* (buying/aid/goals), not a savings rotation.
- vs **group-buying (Pinduoduo):** commerce funnel; Takatuf is values-driven, governed, multi-purpose, bank-trusted.
- vs **Takadao/takaful:** that's mutual *insurance*; Takatuf is general collective action.
- Not on the kill-list.

## J. Risks
1. **Governance disputes / convener trust.** → Rules explicit + bank-enforced + transparent ledger + refund-if-unmet; bank holds funds in amana.
2. **Merchant wholesale integration is the value unlock.** → Demo with a seeded vendor; integration is roadmap.
3. **Could read as "group buying."** → Lead with the *values + governance + mutual-aid* angle, not discounts.
