# 🃏 Wildcard (cross-lane → Vector 3, Faith-Positive): مُعلّق · Mu'allaq
**Suspended giving — the "قهوة معلّقة" tradition, made a real-time, verified, dignity-first rail.**

## One-liner
*Mu'allaq lets anyone pre-pay a **specific real need** — a meal, a child's school fee, a medicine, an overdue utility bill — into a verified pool, redeemed directly at the point of need by someone who qualifies, with full transparency and **complete dignity** (the giver never sees a face; the receiver never asks a person).*

## Why it could win
- **Emotional ceiling is the highest of anything I found** — it operationalizes a beloved Arab/Islamic tradition (the "suspended coffee," sadaqah jariyah) into instant, fraud-resistant, dignity-preserving giving.
- **Only-now/only-here:** Nafath eligibility + merchant point-of-sale integration + sarie + ZATCA-verified merchants → giving tied to a *real fulfilled need*, not a cash transfer that can leak.
- **Shariah-positive** (sadaqah/hibah — pure charity, zero contract debate) and uniquely Saudi in spirit.

## Why it's the wildcard, not my champion
- It's **Faith-Positive (Vector 3 = Agent 3's lane)**, not "a new primitive" → out of my mandate's center.
- It risks colliding with the **national Ehsan platform** (campaign donations) — would need a sharp "point-of-need, dignity-first, person-specific" wedge to differentiate, which is more an *execution* than a *mechanism* leap.

## Hand-off note to Agent 3
If you're working Faith-Positive Finance, **the dignity-first, point-of-need redemption mechanic** (giver funds a *need-category at a specific merchant*; receiver redeems anonymously via Nafath eligibility) is the freshest angle I found vs Ehsan's campaign model. Yours if useful.
