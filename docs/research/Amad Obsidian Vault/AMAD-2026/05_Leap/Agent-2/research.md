# 🔭 Recon — The Invisible Saudis (Phase 1)

Hunting a **category-defining, Shariah-clean** leap for a segment the sector ignores. ~10 live searches; weighted to the richest, most uniquely-Saudi territory: **the Hajj/Umrah pilgrim**, plus accessibility and the aging/illiterate.

## The unserved human truths (by segment)

**Hajj / Umrah pilgrims — the uniquely-Saudi segment no one else on earth can build for**
- Scale: **1.67M Hajj** (2025; 1.51M from 171 countries) + **~18M Umrah/yr**; religious tourism ≈ **$30B/yr ≈ 7% of GDP, ~20% of the non-oil economy.** Avg Hajj spend ~$5,400/pilgrim.
- The pilgrim **wallet is already taken** — **Nusuk Wallet by NEO (Saudi National Bank)**, the "world's first" embedded pilgrim wallet (store funds, installment deposits, card pay during the stay). → **A "pilgrim card/wallet" is dead on arrival and belongs to a rival bank.** The white space is the rest of the journey, which Nusuk does NOT touch: the pilgrim's *obligations*, *family back home*, *legacy*, *spiritual-financial preparation*.
- **The buried truth (the leap):** Islam requires that **before Hajj you clear your debts and return the rights of others (radd al-mazālim)** to attain **بَراءة الذِّمّة** — a clear moral-legal account before God. *"The believer's soul remains suspended by his debt until it is paid."* Pilgrims feel this acutely; **no bank has ever built for it.** It is Shariah-*positive* (serving a core obligation), profoundly emotional, and category-defining.
- Related uniquely-Islamic, unbuilt pre-Hajj acts: writing your **wasiyyah (Islamic will)** + **farāʾiḍ (inheritance shares)** before travel ("a Muslim should not pass two nights without his will written"); discharging unpaid **zakat**; avoiding **isrāf** (wasteful spend) at the sites.

**Accessibility / can't-use-a-screen — the dignity gap**
- Saudi bank apps (AlRajhi, SNB, Riyad — the top 3) **fail WCAG 2.1**; "operable" most-violated. **7.78% of the population has a disability.** Voice-first Arabic banking is essentially absent.
- A sovereign Arabic LLM (**ALLaM**) now makes **screenless, fully-conversational Khaleeji-Arabic banking** possible for the first time — for the blind, the motor-impaired, and the functionally illiterate-with-money (esp. elderly).

**The unbanked / dependent**
- ~**6.9M unbanked adults** (28% of adults; **56% women**, per King Khaled Foundation). Literacy 97.6% overall but lower among elderly/rural. The "invisible" are real and sizable.

## White space (what exists, why the gap)
- **Manual IOU apps** (Splitwise, "Who Owes Me", YouOweMe) exist — but they are **toy notepads**: user-typed, casual, no bank data, no Islamic framing, no settlement, no reaching the rights-holder. **None** auto-discover obligations from real accounts or frame them as *radd al-mazālim / barā'at al-dhimmah*.
- **Faraid/inheritance** tools are thin calculators; no bank ties them to assets + a real will + the pilgrim moment.
- **Pilgrim fintech** = payments only (Nusuk). The *conscience*, *legacy*, and *family* layers are empty.

## The 2026-only capability that makes the leap possible NOW
- **Open Banking (AIS), licensed Mar 2026** → an app can, with consent, read a pilgrim's accounts to surface standing debts, recurring obligations, informal-loan-shaped flows, owed wages/bills.
- **ALLaM via IBM watsonx** → an Arabic conversational agent can *interview* the pilgrim ("whom do you owe? did a relative lend you money? any unpaid promise, wage, or zakat?"), reconstruct a ledger of obligations, and explain rulings in plain Arabic.
- **sarie (instant pay) + Nafath (ID)** → actually *settle* the discovered obligations and verify the people involved, live, before departure.

## Alinma-could-own-this logic
- Alinma is a **Shariah-compliant bank** — only an Islamic bank can credibly own *barā'at al-dhimmah* / *radd al-mazālim* / *farāʾiḍ*. A conventional bank cannot. **This is an uncopyable, faith-positive moat**, and it rides the largest uniquely-Saudi event on earth (Hajj/Umrah) where a rival (SNB) only took the wallet.
- Shippable on Alinma's existing **OB sandbox + ALLaM/watsonx + sarie**; PDPL/consent-clean; **no contested contract** (no Tawarruq, no lending, no chance) — it *moves and settles the customer's own money* and computes obligations. Shariah-clean by design.

## Live-demo data sources
- Alinma OB sandbox (accounts/transactions) + **synthetic pilgrim profiles** with planted obligations (an informal loan from a brother, an unpaid supplier, lapsed zakat, a forgotten BNPL tail).
- A **faraid rule-engine** (deterministic Qur'anic shares) for the will concept.
- ALLaM/watsonx for the Arabic interview + rulings surfacing (human Shariah-board in the loop; never an auto-fatwa).
