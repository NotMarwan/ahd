## B. New attack vectors the depth exposed — each sealed
> Found while going deep; named so they can't ambush us at hour 70.

**Legal / Shariah / Regulatory**
- *"A bank can't be a witness."* → It's the **scribe (كاتب بالعدل)**, 2:282; the parties + Nafath are the attestation. We never claim the bank testifies.
- *"Is this lending? Needs a finance licence?"* → No. Finance = "extending credit under contract"; the **creditor is a private person**, the bank books **zero** receivable. Only regulated touch = payments, on the bank's own rails via the **SAMA sandbox**.
- *"The charity late-fee is still a penalty = riba."* → 100% to audited charity (Ehsan), **never** to lender/bank, **never** to the insolvent (2:280); AAOIFI SS-3-recognized; **opt-in + board-toggle**.
- *"Your erasure rights / retention contradict PDPL."* → Dual lawful basis (consent + contract/legal-claim) + **evidentiary-hold** override, disclosed at signing.
- *"A coerced signature voids the record."* → Biometric, device-bound, **can't pre-sign for another** + a **cooling-off window** + an in-app "signed under pressure" flag → routes to Taradhi; the bank attests the *event*, never *voluntariness*.
- *"A money-mule will launder through it."* → Both sides **Nafath-KYC'd**, money only on KYC'd bank rails, velocity/structuring monitoring into the bank's AML pipeline; the relationship graph is itself an AML signal. Better than cash.
- *"The AI will draft a haram term / issue a fatwa."* → Constrained to **board-approved templates** + a deterministic **riba lint**; novel cases **escalate to a human**; "**ليست فتوى**" stated on screen.

**Technical / Security**
- *"Your hash is theatre."* → It's the **SEAL**: SHA-256 (integrity) + Nafath-bound assertions (attribution) + RFC-3161 (time) + chain & bank signature (non-repudiation, even against the bank). Four court needs, not one.
- *"Saying it's admissible doesn't make it admissible."* → Correct — we **engineer the integrity properties** the law requires and claim "**built to be admissible**," with sign-off disclosed, not asserted.
- *"So the bank IS liable as a witness."* → The record **names the four things the bank refuses to vouch for** (cash moved, fairness, voluntariness, underlying truth). It witnesses the *signing event*, never the *debt*.
- *"Your trust score is an illegal credit score."* → No number, no ranking, never underwrites, never reaches SIMAH, never leaves the dyad — structurally incapable of being a credit score.

**Behavioral / Growth**
- *"It's used twice a year — no retention."* → Salaries, rent, installments are **monthly**; the recurring-covenant object → ~18 events/yr, ~3× D30. The third open is the **dignity of watching a ذِمّة close.**
- *"The borrower has no reason to join."* → The borrower gets the one thing a verbal IOU never gives: **the right to be finally, witnessed-ly settled**, with an iron guarantee of **zero riba, zero penalty**. Signing protects the borrower *more* than the lender.

**Product / Demo**
- *"Muqassa minimizes transfers? Counterexample."* → We **don't** claim the minimum (NP-hard); we claim **≤ P−1 transfers** (each greedy step zeroes ≥1 party), **O(E + P log P)** — NOT "each party once" (a star pattern refutes it, per verification C15).
- *"You're netting debts between people who never agreed."* → **Opt-in, Nafath-consented per member**; a decline → bilateral fallback; netting is a witnessed **ahd-of-ahds** (classical *مقاصّة*).
- *"Show me a defaulted/disputed agreement."* → One seeded click; **DEFAULTED carries no penalty** (riba); **DISPUTED exports** a deterministic evidence bundle to Najiz/Taradhi. Record-keeper, never adjudicator.
