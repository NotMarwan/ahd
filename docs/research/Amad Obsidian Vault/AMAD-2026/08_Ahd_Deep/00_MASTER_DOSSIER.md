## 0. The one-paragraph thesis

**Ahd turns the most common and least-served financial act on earth — money between two people — into a fair, plain-Arabic, interest-free (qard hasan) agreement that Alinma drafts as the *just scribe* (الكاتب بالعدل of Ayat al-Dayn, 2:282), seals as court-admissible electronic evidence (a Nafath-identity-bound, SHA-256 hash-chained, trusted-timestamped *iqrār bil-dayn* under Evidence Law 2022, where the denier bears the burden), and auto-settles over sarie — collapsing a circle's tangled IOUs into a few consented transfers (≤ P−1) with Muqassa.** The bank lends nothing, judges nothing, and charges nothing on the loan: it earns a flat actual-direct-cost *wakala/amana* service fee (and float, deposits, and viral acquisition) as a record-keeper and settlement agent — a posture that is Shariah-clean by construction, regulatorily light (no new credit activity; in-flight funds handled as safeguarded funds on the bank's own licensed rails), and a brand moat only an Islamic bank can credibly own. It is **not** "viral by construction" — organic k ≲ 0.36 (realistically ~0.10–0.36) — so growth is honest: an organic referral loop, **amplified inside dense circles** by Muqassa, and **on-ramped** by the mass Nafath/wallet/KYC presence that the Jan-2026 domestic-worker e-salary mandate guarantees (a friction-lowering adjacency, *not* a two-sided rail by itself). And every design choice converts the one fatal objection — *"asking someone you love to sign an IOU feels like distrust"* — into the product's emotional core: the lender signs first and *gives*, so the counterparty receives a deed, not a demand, and both end at **ذِمّة محفوظة** — the obligation kept, the relationship intact.

## 1. The spine (one product, one identity — do not drift)

**Ahd is the first bank-witnessed rail for money between people.** A loan to a friend, a family IOU, a flatmate's share, a deferred promise becomes a **fair, plain-Arabic, qard-hasan agreement** that Alinma:
- **writes** (AI-drafted from board-approved templates — the scribe),
- **witnesses** (dual Nafath-authenticated, licensed-TSP e-signature),
- **records** (tamper-evident, court-admissible *iqrār* under Evidence Law 2022),
- **settles** (auto, over sarie), and
- **nets** (Muqassa — a circle's tangled debts collapse to a few consented transfers).

**The bank lends nothing.** It is *amīn* (trustee), *wakīl* (settlement agent), and *kātib* (scribe) — **never lender, guarantor, or judge.** Disputes exit to MoJ / Najiz / Taradhi; Ahd hands over the evidence and stops.

## 2. The problem — a civilization-old command meeting a sector that ignored it

The most common financial act between humans has **no product**. It runs on memory and trust, and breaks both:
- **Saudis already document personal debt — but with a blunt, term-less instrument.** The **سند لأمر (promissory note) is among the most widely used legal documents in the Kingdom**, directly enforceable through **Najiz** — yet it carries no qard-hasan structure, no schedule, no settlement, and is so abuse-prone that **SAMA had to bar finance firms from demanding it** from consumers. The documentation *habit* already exists; a fair, structured, Shariah-clean instrument does not. *(rhn-group; ahysp.com; SAMA Rulebook.)*
- **The justice system is saturated with debt/documentation traffic:** the Ministry of Justice delivered **43M+ Najiz e-services in H1 2024** — *enforcement, documentation, and acknowledgment (إقرار)* among the categories. *(Arab News; SPA.)*
- **Illustrative relational pain (US proxy — KSA figure pending):** ~50% of lenders never write the loan down; ~30% of borrowers never fully repay; ~1 in 6 say money has *ruined* a relationship. *(LendingTree / Bankrate — **US surveys**; re-source to KSA primary data pre-pitch — §11.)*
- Banks treat it as out-of-scope. **Splitwise** only *counts* (and has **no Arabic**). **sarie** moves money but records no *terms*. **Najiz** notarizes property/POAs, not casual interpersonal money.
- Yet a billion Muslims are scripturally instructed to **record and witness their debts** — *Ayat al-Dayn*, **Quran 2:282**, the **longest verse in the Quran**: *"…when you contract a debt for a fixed term, write it down… and let a scribe write it between you in justice."* No one ever built the product for the verse.

**The scale is not niche.** Cross-border interpersonal remittances alone were **SAR 144.2B (expat) + SAR 68.6B (Saudi-national) ≈ SAR 213B in 2024** (SAMA) — before a riyal of *domestic* family/friend lending or rent-splitting.

## 3. The solution & why it is only-possible-now and only-here

Two people create an *ahd*: pick the kind (قرض حسن / shared cost / deferred / promise-gift) → the AI drafts fair, riba-clean Arabic terms from board-approved templates → both confirm via Nafath → a tamper-evident, admissible record is sealed → Alinma auto-settles via sarie and, on completion, marks both parties' **ذِمّة محفوظة**. Within a circle, **Muqassa** nets the tangle.

| Enabler | What it makes possible | Reality (verified) |
|---|---|---|
| **Evidence Law 2022** (RD M/43) | a digital record is admissible documentary evidence; the *denier bears the burden* (Arts. 55–59) | presumptive/rebuttable, judicial determination |
| **Nafath + licensed TSP** (emdha-class) | gov-grade identity (auth) → AES/QES signature | Nafath = auth; the *signature* is the TSP's |
| **sarie** (SAMA IPS) | instant 24/7 settlement | SAR 20k/txn cap; credit-push (no silent pull) |
| **ALLaM / watsonx** | plain-Arabic term drafting | assist only — never a fatwa |
| **Ayat al-Dayn (2:282)** | the cultural/spiritual mandate to document debt | 1,400 years old |

Could **not** have been built two years ago (no Evidence-Law admissibility, no mature Nafath e-sign), and only makes cultural sense **here first.**

## 4. The four-layer depth (the fused argument)

### 4.1 Legal · Shariah · Regulatory → [[Agent-1/layer-legal-shariah-regulatory]]
- **The scribe doctrine (keystone).** 2:282 names *two* roles: the **scribe** (كاتب بالعدل) and the **witnesses** (شهيدان). **Alinma is the scribe;** the *witnesses* are the **Nafath-attested parties + the record.** A corporation cannot testify — and never claims to. This resolves "a bank can't be a witness" with the literal division of labour in the verse.
- **Admissibility, constructed.** A two-law stack: **ETL 2007** (signature equivalence — Art. 14; record integrity — Art. 8) + **Evidence Law 2022** (digital-evidence weight, Arts. 55–59; **burden on the contesting party**, Art. 58; **judicial determination**, Arts. 4/61/62). The signed **iqrār bil-dayn** is among the strongest proofs in Islamic procedure. Evidence is **presumptive/rebuttable**, and full certified-signature weight requires an **accredited CSP/TSA** — stated honestly, claim ceiling "*designed to meet the admissibility conditions*," never "is admissible."
- **Riba-safe fee, two levels.** (1) The bank is **not a party** to the qard → its fee is **wakala/amana** *ujrah* for a separate service, not a charge on the loan. (2) Even under AAOIFI **SS-19**, the fee is **flat, actual-DIRECT-cost, independent of principal and tenor** — never %, never time-scaled, never a late fee. The contestable part is that the service must be **genuine and independent** (not a precondition of the qard — SS-19 cl.7/8, Hilah doctrine), so the **conservative default is free at the consumer layer, float-monetised**, and the figure/methodology need **Alinma's own board** (premise stated conditionally).
- **Halal enforcement (the sharpest hole, sealed).** Ladder: (1) evidentiary (the admissible record); (2) social (private kept-word); (3) optional, board-gated **iltizām bi-l-tabarruʿ** (fixed charity pledge to Ehsan, **never** to lender/bank — AAOIFI SS-3). The insolvent (*muʿsir*) get **mandated grace** (2:280), never a penalty. Primary posture: **DEFAULTED carries no penalty** — it only changes reminder tone and unlocks export.
- **SAMA fit.** Finance = "extending credit under contract"; the **creditor is a private individual**, the bank books **zero receivable** → no finance licence. The regulated touch is **payments**: in-flight funds are **safeguarded funds** (PSP-grade duties), run **as agent on Alinma's own licensed rails**, launched via the **time-boxed SAMA Sandbox** with an exit plan.
- **Plus:** the fatwa firewall (AI constrained to board templates + a deterministic riba lint; never rules); the fiqh edge-case table (gift-vs-loan, death/estate, insolvency, capacity, coercion, currency, waʿd, zakat); PDPL dual-basis + evidentiary-hold-vs-erasure; the prohibited-claims list; board-validatable Arabic **term templates**; and the **graded-trust tiers** (T0 note / T1 light / T2 full AES) — the legal half of the cold-start fix.

### 4.2 Technical · Security · Data → [[Agent-2/layer-tech-security]]
- **The SEAL** replaces the demo's 32-bit FNV with the five properties a court needs: **(1)** canonicalize (RFC 8785 JCS), **(2)** SHA-256 (integrity), **(3)** signer-bind the Nafath assertion `sub/acr/auth_time/txn_id` (attribution + WYSIWYS), **(4)** RFC-3161 timestamp (time), **(5)** hash-chain + bank signature + periodic signed Merkle checkpoints (non-repudiation, even against the bank). Money is **integer halalas**, never floats.
- **The attestation boundary, in the bytes.** The record states what the bank attests (two Nafath identities sealed *this byte-string* at *this time*; unaltered) and the four things it does **not** vouch for (cash moved, fairness, voluntariness, underlying truth). "You witnessed it, so you're liable" dies on contact.
- **Threat model** (the dominant adversary is an *insider party*): designed defenses for coercion, fabricated agreements, repudiation, money-muling, Muqassa collusion, replay, account takeover, and a lender inflating terms (T1–T8).
- **Muqassa, specified and proven.** Greedy debtor↔creditor reduction, **O(E + n log n)**, **net-preservation invariant** (every party settles their exact net), bounded **≤ P−1 transfers** (each iteration zeroes ≥1 party) — claimed as "**few/efficient transfers**," explicitly **not** "the minimum" (NP-hard, disclosed with a counterexample) and **not** "each party pays once" (a star pattern refutes it). Executes only as a **consented novation** (every affected party re-confirms before any sarie transfer).
- **The trust signal — provably not a credit score.** A windowed, decayed *kept-ratio* over the user's **own sealed history only**, shown as a 3-band qualitative mirror — never a number, never sold, never an underwriting input, never to SIMAH. The **/01 Data** depth as genuine computation, and the compliance shield in one move.

### 4.3 Behavioral · Adoption · Growth → [[Agent-3/layer-growth-adoption]]
- **The lender-signs-first inversion (the cold-start keystone).** The lender drafts, *gives*, and signs **first**; the invite arrives as *"Noura honoured her word and gave you 5,000 — qard hasan, zero interest, confirm receipt"* — a **gift-receipt, not an accusation.**
- **Honest k — and the honest fix.** Organic **k ≲ 0.36** (realistic ~0.10–0.36; app-invite links are the lowest-converting referral class) → Ahd is **not** "viral by construction." Growth is (a) the organic loop, (b) **circle-amplified** by Muqassa (local k>1 in dense groups), and (c) **on-ramped** by the **Jan-2026 domestic-worker e-salary mandate** — which guarantees **mass employer+worker wallet/KYC presence** and a recurring **salary use-case**, *lowering onboarding friction for an adjacent covenant product.* **Honest correction (verification C10):** that mandate is a *one-sided* employer→closed-wallet rail; it does **not** by itself create a two-sided interpersonal rail or solve k<1 — the two-sided GTM is separate work. Regulatory-driven presence is reported **distinctly from organic k.**
- **The cultural reframe as a copy doctrine** (five rules + exact on-screen Arabic at every beat) — sutures *"signing feels like سوء الظن"* into *"توثيق الدَّين سُنّة تحفظ المودّة."*
- **The retention engine.** Recurring/scheduled covenants (rent, salary, installments) turn episodic lending into monthly touchpoints (~4 → ~18 events/yr); the **covenant ledger**; and a **private kept-word reputation**. Modeled D1 55% / D7 35% / D30 22% (recurring hook ~triples D30).
- **The beachhead ladder:** salary covenants (riding the mass wallet/KYC presence) → flatmate rent/bills (dense, Muqassa-native) → family lending (the emotional heart) → group trips/weddings → casual promises.
