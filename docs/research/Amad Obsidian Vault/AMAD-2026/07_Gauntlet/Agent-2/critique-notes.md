# 🧭 Agent 2 — gap map → why Kanaf & Sanad

My full teardown of the portfolio is in [[teardown]] (I seeded it; sharpest on **Ahd**, my arena's incumbent). The two recurring portfolio holes:
- **Cold-start** — two-sided, or culturally cold (Ahd: formalizing a friend-loan signals distrust), or low-frequency (Dhimmah seasonal; Athar/Mu'allaq discretionary giving).
- **"So-what"** — discretionary / nice-to-have rather than urgent + recurring.

**The unowned high-ground in The Social Fabric:** money-between-people that is **(1) naturally multi-party** (the group pre-exists → no cold-start), **(2) emotionally warm or urgent** (duty/love/crisis, not a suspicious IOU), **(3) high-stakes + recurring** (not a one-off), and **(4) structurally bank-only** (an app cannot hold a family's trust or enforce a distributed guarantee).

Two directions hit this ground from opposite sides:
- **[[concept-kanaf|كَنَف Kanaf]]** — *money flowing DOWN to those you must care for* (parents/dependents): siblings co-provide, fairly, with dignity. Warm, recurring, religiously mandated (نفقة/بِرّ). **Track 2 (CX).**
- **[[concept-sanad|سَنَد Sanad]]** — *trust flowing TO someone who needs access*: your circle's distributed **guarantee (kafāla)** unlocks housing/work/education for the credit-invisible, no lending. Urgent, high-stakes, inclusion. **Track 5 (Open Banking).**

Different tracks, different mechanics (provision-pool vs guarantee-primitive), different money-direction — not two variants. Both **surpass Ahd** by sealing its exact holes, and both are **Shariah-positive** (نفقة/بِرّ; kafāla/ḍamān) not merely clean.

De-confliction vs Agent 4 (Arena 4) and their finalists: Kanaf ≠ Amana (escrow) / Takatuf (generic pool) — it's *directed family provision with fair-share + dignity*. Sanad ≠ lending/Tadfuq (retired) — the bank lends nothing; it's a *distributed guarantee*, money moves only on default.
