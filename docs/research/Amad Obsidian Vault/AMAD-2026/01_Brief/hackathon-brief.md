# AMAD 2026 — Hackathon Brief (canonical)

**What:** Fintech hackathon — build a digital solution for the Saudi financial sector.
**Partners:** Alinma Bank (Shariah-compliant Islamic bank) × Tuwaiq Academy.
**Where/when:** 72h onsite, Tuwaiq Academy HQ, Riyadh. **16–18 July 2026.** Enrichment 5–16 July (hybrid).
**Registration closes:** **26 June 2026** (hard gate — initial idea + team of 3–5).
**Prizes:** 1st = **250,000 SAR**, 2nd = 150,000, 3rd = 100,000.
**Finance background:** not required. **Final judging:** live pitch + working demo, 18 July.

## 6 Tracks (pick ONE per concept)
1. Generative AI for FinTech
2. Customer Experience
3. Financial Regulations (RegTech)
4. Financial Education
5. Open Banking
6. Gamification

## 4 Requirements (satisfy ≥1)
- /01 Data Analysis · /02 Use of AI · /03 Improve Customer Experience · /04 Sustainable Financial Solutions

## 5 Evaluation Criteria (the rubric)
1. Innovation & creativity · 2. Technical implementation · 3. Data analysis · 4. UX · 5. Real-world feasibility in finance

**Strategic read:** Criterion 5 (feasibility — "Alinma could ship this") is hardest to fake, weighted highest. /01 Data + /02 AI together cover 3 of 5 criteria.
