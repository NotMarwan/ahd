## 2. The orphaned holes — integrator's answers

### A2 — "Why Alinma and not Al Rajhi?" (the FATAL one)
**Concede the red-team is right:** the tech is copyable in weeks, and the *religious-category* moat ("only an Islamic bank can own qard hasan / 2:282") is shared by Al Rajhi, Albilad, and Alinma's peers. So the firm-specific moat is **not** the tech and **not** "being Islamic." It is three things Alinma must **deliberately** build, and the pitch must say so:
1. **Category land-grab + brand association.** Be *first* to make "the bank is the **just scribe** of your word." Owning the name **عهد** and the 2:282 association in an emotional category is sticky — second movers look derivative. Emotional-brand primacy is a real moat where feature-parity isn't.
2. **≥1 exclusive distribution partnership** for a *seeded, two-sided* cohort — the thing a fast-follower can't instantly copy. Concretely: an **HRSD/Musaned wage-protection integration** (worker-salary covenants) **or** a major **property-management / student-housing chain** (flatmate rent). This simultaneously kills **A5** (names the channel + a contracted seed) and **A2** (a partnership is exclusive in a way code is not).
3. **Circle network-lock-in.** Once a family/trip/flat **circle's** covenants live on Alinma's Ahd, switching means moving the *whole circle* — a genuine network effect that compounds with first-mover.
**The honest strategic line for the pitch:** *"This is a category land-grab. Al Rajhi can copy the rails in a month — which is exactly why Alinma must define the category, own the name, and lock a distribution partner **now.**"* Silence on Al Rajhi loses the round; this answer wins it.

### A8 — depth mis-allocation
Keep the audit-grade depth **in the dossier** (it's the §11 win-condition narrative — it survives the SAMA/Shariah/Nafath review that real deployment needs). But the **demo and the slide budget** must re-weight to what Track 2 (CX) actually scores: **proof of demand + emotional warmth.** [[demo-v2]] swaps one dense crypto beat for the C1 gift-receipt + C2 يُسر beats; the SEAL/tamper-toggle stays but compressed.

### A9 — Nafath-use permission
Flag honestly as a pre-production validation item (not a demo claim): confirm with SDAIA / the licensed TSP that **interpersonal-debt witnessing** is an approved AES purpose. Until confirmed, the demo says "Nafath identity + a licensed signature provider" and never asserts the permission is granted.
