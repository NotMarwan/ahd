# 🤍 Ahd — Embrace Additions · the touches that make a Saudi feel *held*

> **Agent 3 · Consumer Soul.** The ranked list of additions that turn Ahd from a thing that *works* into a thing a user *loves* and feels protected by — so they open it again and tell their family. Each: **the need · the emotional payoff · a concrete sketch (real Arabic microcopy) · build difficulty · [demo-now] / [v2].** Ranked by **felt-warmth per build-hour** (Agent 4's selection metric). Derived from the lived journeys in [[consumer-journey]].
>
> **Guardrails baked in:** on-spine (bank = scribe/witness, never lender/judge); Shariah-clean (warmth never becomes manipulation, maysir, or riba); **never shame a late payer**; **never imply legal certainty** (keep «مهيّأة كدليل… القبول تقدير القاضي»); reputation stays **private**; AI drafts, never issues a fatwa.

## ⭐ TIER 1 — the soul (build these for the demo; they ARE the round)

### 1. «وصلتك بسلامة» — the gift-receipt invite screen  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Medium**
*The single highest-value addition. It's the make-or-break beat and it currently has no screen (journey C1).*
- **Need:** the borrower (خالد, سارة, رهف) must receive the ask as *care*, not an accusation. Today they're a card that gets tapped on the lender's device.
- **Emotional payoff:** the moment that decides whether the product heals or wounds the relationship. Done right, the borrower feels *given to and protected*, and joins without resentment.
- **Sketch:** a distinct "second-person" screen (the link target) — **deed first, protection explicit, obligation last**:
  > **«نورة وفّت بوعدها وأعطتك ٥٠٠٠ ريال.»**
  > قرضٌ حسن — **بلا أي فائدة، وبلا غرامة تأخير**. سدّد بالراحة المتّفق عليها، ﴿وإن كان ذو عسرة فنظرة إلى ميسرة﴾.
  > كلمةٌ محفوظة بينكما — لا حسابات، لا إحراج.
  > `[ أكّد استلامي بسلامة ]   [ عندي ملاحظة ]`
  On confirm: **«وصلتك بسلامة 🤍 — عهدٌ موثّق»** (not a cold "✓ مؤكَّد").
- **Demo move:** after نورة signs first, the screen **flips to خالد's phone view** ("the borrower's side") and shows this — the judges *feel* the inversion the dossier only describes. Pairs perfectly with Agent 2's live riba-linter (the "بلا فائدة" is *shown* being enforced).

### 2. «متى ما تيسّر» — the يُسر safety net (what if I can't pay?)  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Medium**
*The deepest "this app has my back" feeling. Absent today (journey C2).*
- **Need:** the borrower's #1 fear (*what if I stumble?*) and the lender's #1 awkwardness (*how do I ask without ruining it?*). The settle screen currently pays perfectly every time — the human reality of a hard month has no home.
- **Emotional payoff:** the borrower signs **without fear**; the mother (فاطمة) knows the app will protect her son's dignity if he struggles; the friendship is never the collector. This is the most Islamic beat in the product (2:280 grace > 2:282 documentation).
- **Sketch:**
  - *At signing (reassurance):* «إن تعسّر السداد يومًا، تُعاد الجدولة بالمعروف — لا فائدة، لا غرامة، لا حرج. ﴿وإن كان ذو عسرة فنظرة إلى ميسرة﴾.»
  - *On the settle screen, a real button beside each installment:* `🌿 أحتاج وقت` → opens **reschedule-together**, framed as ease: «نُعيد توزيع المتبقّي بالمعروف — متى ما تيسّر.» Outcome state = **«مؤجّل بالتراضي»**, never «متأخر».
  - The lender's side shows it gently: «خالد يحتاج مهلة بسيطة — وهذا من المعروف.» (informs, never weaponizes).
- **Demo move:** a 5-second beat — tap «أحتاج وقت» → the schedule re-spreads, no penalty appears, the riba-clean invariant visibly holds. Answers the judge's "what about defaults?" with *dignity* instead of *enforcement*.

### 3. الإشهاد، لا المحكمة — re-tone the witnessing from courtroom to covenant  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low–Medium**
*Keep the tech the judges love; remove the distrust it currently radiates (journey C3).*
- **Need:** screen 2 leads with «دليلٌ لا يقبل العبث» and «جرّب العبث بالمبلغ» — a litigation frame that whispers *we're preparing for when one of you cheats.*
- **Emotional payoff:** witnessing (إشهاد) becomes an act of *honouring a word*, the way the Quran frames it — warm, not adversarial. Judges still get the tamper-proof wow, just one tap deeper.
- **Sketch:** headline → **«نُوثّق كلمتكما لتبقى — شاهدٌ أمين، لا حَكَم.»** The tamper-proof verifier moves behind a calm reveal: `🛡️ كيف نحفظها؟` → expands to the SHA-256 verifier + «جرّب العبث» (now framed as *"see how safe it is"*, not *"try cheating"*). Reaffirm the boundary warmly: «عهد يكتب ويشهد ويحفظ — ولا يحكم بينكما. عند الخلاف، السجلّ جاهز للقضاء.»
- **Spine note:** reinforces "scribe not judge" — strengthens the legal story *and* the warmth at once.

### 4. «معروفك يُحفظ» — the giving-intention beat + dua micro-moments  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low**
*Cheapest warmth in the app; honors the act instead of processing it (journey C4, C5).*
- **Need:** the lender's kindness is currently rendered as a data-entry form. The act of qard hasan — among the most rewarded acts in Islam — is never acknowledged.
- **Emotional payoff:** the giver feels *seen and blessed*, not processed. Sets a warm tone from screen 1 instead of minute 3.
- **Sketch:**
  - *Before the create form:* «أنتِ على وشك أن تصنعي معروفًا. **«كلُّ قرضٍ صدقة»** — و«عهد» يحفظ كلمتكما حتى تبقى المودّة.» `[ لنوثّق المعروف ]`
  - *Micro-duas, gentle, never preachy (one touch, never a sermon):* on issuing → «جزاكِ الله خيرًا — معروفكِ موثّق 🤍»; on a settled installment → «دفعةٌ محفوظة. تقبّل الله.»
- **Guardrail:** exactly one faith touch per beat; opt-out of duas in settings (warmth, never pressure).

## ⭐ TIER 2 — high-leverage delight (each is a small build, big feeling)

### 5. Show the gentle reminder (don't just promise it)  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low**
- **Need:** "بلا تذكير محرج" is asserted; the user's fear ("will it nag my mother / embarrass me?") is never answered (journey C6).
- **Payoff:** trust that the app is a *kind trustee*, not a debt-collector — removes the biggest adoption fear.
- **Sketch:** a previewable rail-sent notification: **«تذكير لطيف من عهد: قسطك مع نورة بعد ٣ أيام — متى ما تيسّر 🌿»** — and a one-line promise: «التذكير من «عهد»، لا منكما — فلا تكونان أبدًا في موضع المُطالِب.» (Rail-asks-never-the-lender, [[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.9.)

### 6. «ذمّة محفوظة» elevated — shared, blessed, and remembered  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low–Medium**
- **Need:** the one warm beat is solo and one-shot (journey C10). It can be the emotional peak.
- **Payoff:** *both* parties feel the relationship survived intact; the giver gets a private dignity stamp that pulls them back (retention Driver 3).
- **Sketch:** both phones show **«ذمّةٌ محفوظة — وفّيتما بعهدكما، وبقيت المودّة. 🤝»** + a soft dua «بارك الله فيكما» + an optional kind note back («تسلمين يا نورة»). Private kept-word stamp: **«هذا عهدك الـ١٢ الذي وُفِّي به — كلمتك محفوظة.»** (self-facing only — never shown to others; honors [[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.8).

### 7. Muqassa = relief, not a spreadsheet  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low**
- **Need:** the netting delivers "math" («برهان الحفظ»), not the *feeling* of relief (journey C7).
- **Payoff:** the actual win — *nobody has to chase anybody* — lands emotionally.
- **Sketch:** before the proof table: **«انفكّ التشابك — ما بقي أحدٌ يطالب أحدًا. بدل ٩ تحويلاتٍ محرجة بين الأصدقاء، تحويلان فقط.»** Keep the conservation proof underneath for the auditors; lead with the human line.

### 8. Reputation as a private leaf, not a rating over your friends  ·  [demo-now]  ·  build: **Low**
- **Need:** the kept-ratio **%** floating over friends' heads reads like a credit score of your friends — the one thing the product swears it isn't (journey C8).
- **Payoff:** keeps the warmth of "kept word" without the judgment; protects the "not a credit score" promise *emotionally*, not just legally.
- **Sketch:** in the circle graph, drop the «٨٧٪» labels over *other* people; show only a quiet **🌿 سجلّ وفاء** that each user sees for **themselves**. If a trust signal must appear to a counterparty, use a 3-band qualitative word («وفيّ»), never a number ([[layer-growth-adoption]] §3.8). Caption: «سمعةُ وفاءٍ خاصّة بك — ليست تقييمًا لأحد، وليست تصنيفًا ائتمانيًا.»

### 9. «بالكلام البسيط» + اسمعيها — plain-language & read-aloud terms  ·  [demo-now (toggle) / v2 (TTS)]  ·  build: **Low / Medium-High**
- **Need:** an older lender (فاطمة, 52) faces small, formal legal Arabic and feels excluded (journey C9). Inclusion *is* dignity.
- **Payoff:** every generation can use it confidently — the app meets people where they are.
- **Sketch:** a toggle on the terms: `📖 بالكلام البسيط` → «نورة أعطت خالد ٥٠٠٠، يردّها على ٥ أشهر، بدون أي زيادة — ومتى ما تعسّر، في مهلة.» Plus `🔊 اسمعيها` (read-aloud) for v2.

## 💫 TIER 3 — «لو صارت بتكون مجنونة» (big dreams · all [v2])

- **دائرة العائلة / الرحلة — persistent circles.** A standing family or trip space where mutual covenants live and Muqassa runs on demand; the warm frame: «أمانة العائلة — محفوظة.» (Builds on §3.10 circle-seeding.)
- **العفو كعبادة — forgiveness, done warmly (giver-initiated only).** When a debt completes or a borrower struggles, *offer the lender* (never the borrower, never as shame): «هل تحبّ أن تعفو عن المتبقّي صدقةً؟ ﴿وأن تصدّقوا خيرٌ لكم﴾.» Turns the AAOIFI charity-pledge idea into an act of worship — **strictly the giver's choice**, never a penalty, never visible pressure on the borrower.
- **«كلمتك هذا العام» — a yearly dignity wrap.** «وفّيت بـ١٤ عهدًا هذه السنة — كلمتك محفوظة.» Private, returning ritual.
- **شهادة وفاء قابلة للإهداء — an opt-in, scoped trust badge** a user *chooses* to show one prospective counterparty (like a reference, PDPL-consented) — never public, never a score.
- **توثيق صوتي للأمّيّين — voice-first covenant** for low-literacy users: hear the terms, confirm by Nafath — radical inclusion.

## The 2 I'd build if I could build only 2
**#1 (the gift-receipt invite screen)** and **#2 (the يُسر safety net).** Together they are the product's soul: *receiving care instead of an accusation*, and *being protected when you stumble*. They convert Ahd from "a witnessed-debt tool" into "the app that has your back." Everything else is delight on top.
