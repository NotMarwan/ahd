# 💥 Phase 2 — Diverge (≥20, force past the obvious)
> Gut checks 1–5: **S** = surprise ("never seen this"), **E** = emotional truth, **F** = 72h-demo feasibility. Leap bar (§4) needs high S + E, not just F.

## 🪦 The safe 3 — DISCARDED as the price of entry (these are Round-1-caliber)
- **D1. A better zakat calculator / auto-zakat.** Commoditized (Ehsan, ZATCA Zakaty) + reeks of retired Rushd. Killed.
- **D2. A donation app with nicer UX / streaks.** Spend-down, and a reward-streak = retired Namaa in disguise. Killed.
- **D3. A faraid calculator.** A dozen exist for free. A calculator is a feature, not a leap. Killed (kept only if made *living + notarized* → see #2).

## Converge — the 3 that best pass the leap bar (surprise + emotion + category), not the 3 most feasible
1. **[[concept-athar|أثر Athar]]** (#1, absorbs 7/8/10/11/20) — highest S+E; *creates a category* (consumer perpetual waqf). The leap.
2. **[[concept-wasiyyah|وصية Wasiyyah]]** (#2, absorbs #17) — a *different deed* (leave rightly); only-Islamic-bank; differentiates from commoditized calculators via living + Nafath-notarized.
3. **[[concept-yusr|يُسر Yusr]]** (#3, absorbs 12/13) — a *different deed* (lift each other); the anti-riba; emotionally huge.

### Why the cuts were cut
- #4/#9 family/will-waqf → folded into Athar (gifting/dedication) + Wasiyyah (waqf-in-will).
- #5 Ajr ledger → too abstract to demo; risks "measuring reward" theological overreach.
- #14 zakat→waqf → fiqh-contested (zakat must reach the poor directly) → fails clean-by-design.
- #19 sukuk-for-good → lower surprise; it's a sub-mechanism of Athar.
- #21 waqf-as-API → great platform vision but B2B, low 3-min wow → roadmap for Athar.
- #16/#22 pilgrim/ritual → Lane 2's turf (Invisible Saudis); #22 kept as the cross-lane [[wildcard]].
