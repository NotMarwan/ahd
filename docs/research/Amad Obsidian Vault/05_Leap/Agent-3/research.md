# 🔭 Phase 1 Recon — Faith-Positive Finance
> The leap mandate: make Shariah a *superpower*, uncontested-by-design. 10 live searches, deep in lane. Shared intel → [[saudi-fintech-terrain]].

## The unserved human truth (the emotional core)
- The most-quoted hadith on legacy: *"When the son of Adam dies, his deeds cease except three — a **sadaqah jariyah** (ongoing charity), beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for him."* Every practicing Saudi wants the first one. **Today there is no consumer product that lets an ordinary person create one.** You can *donate* (spend-down) — you cannot easily *endow* (give forever).
- **Waqf = "the sleeping giant of Islamic finance"** (~US$1T globally, heading toward a US$5–7.5T Islamic-finance market by 2030) — but it's **tech-starved** and, historically, **only for the wealthy** (you endow a building, a farm). The ordinary believer is locked out of the single most rewarded act in Islamic giving. [IFN — sleeping giant](https://www.islamicfinancenews.com/the-waqf-industry-the-sleeping-giant-of-islamic-finance.html)
- Two adjacent faith-duties are equally underserved at the *connected* level: **faraid/inheritance** (a religious obligation almost no Saudi has planned) and **qard hasan** (the believer's duty to lift each other interest-free).

## What exists — and the gap it leaves
- **Donation platforms (spend-down):** Saudi **Ehsan** — SAR **9bn+** donated, **4.8M** beneficiaries, **3M+** users, **10-second** giving. Brilliant rails — but every riyal is *consumed once*. [Ehsan/SPA](https://spa.gov.sa/en/N2064749) → **Gap: endowment, not donation.**
- **Institutional waqf digitization:** AWQAF × **Naseej** (digital portal/app, Apr-2025); **"Awqaf PAY"**; the **Jood Waqf Fund digital endowment certificate**, instantly issued on contribution (Feb-2026). [Naseej](https://naseej.com/news/2025/04/general-authority-for-awqaf-and-naseej-to-drive-digital-transformation-in-the-endowment-sector/) · [Jood cert](https://www.gccbusinessnews.com/jood-waqf-fund-digital-endowment) → **All institutional/registration/payments — none is a personal, AI-managed, perpetual *micro*-endowment with living impact.**
- **Faraid:** dozens of free calculators (HalalWallet, Al-Wirasat, IslamicInheritance.com) + will-drafting (ShariaWiz). [HalalWallet](https://www.halalwallet.us/tools/faraid-calculator) → **A static calculator is commoditized; the gap is a *living, net-worth-connected, Nafath-notarized* estate that updates with life events.**
- **Qard hasan:** nascent platforms (Qard Al-Hassan community pools; KSA fintech "experimenting" with SAR 1–10k benevolent micro-loans). [investriyadh](https://investriyadh.ai/glossary/qard-hasan/) → **No bank-grade, AI-matched, recycling mutual-aid network exists.**

## The 2026 capability that makes the leap possible NOW
- **Cash waqf + tokenized/fractional halal income assets** make *micro*-endowment viable for the first time: ADIB **Smart Sukuk** already automates onboarding + profit distribution for small retail amounts; world's-first **waqf-featured ETF** routes halal dividends to waqf causes; **Finterra "Waqf Chain."** [White&Case](https://www.whitecase.com/insight-our-thinking/tokenised-islamic-finance-products-shariah-compliance-meets-digital-innovation) → the principal can be parked in an **equity-based halal endowment fund** (no Tawarruq, no riba) whose returns give forever.
- **Licensed Open Banking** (AIS) to fund/round-up; **Nafath** to legally register a waqf or notarize a wasiyyah (the AWQAF digital-certificate precedent proves it); **ALLaM via watsonx** for Arabic guidance + impact narration; **Ehsan-style verified beneficiaries** for the living-impact feed.

## Data / APIs for a real live demo
- AWQAF endowment-investment-fund return rates (public) → the perpetual-projection model. Ehsan beneficiary categories → the impact feed. OB sandbox (AIS) → funding + round-ups. Nafath mock → the registration step. Synthetic donor + impact data for determinism. A real, downloadable return rate + a real hadith on screen = the data story + the emotional beat.

## "Alinma could OWN this" logic
- Alinma is a **Shariah bank** — only an Islamic bank can credibly launch **"Alinma Waqf."** It creates a **new product category + sticky endowed AUM** (the principal sits with the bank, in perpetuity), a **Vision-2030 / AWQAF-aligned** halo, and a partnership-ready hook with the **General Authority for Awqaf**. The global waqf giant is asleep and tech-starved → **Saudi/Alinma could lead the region and the world** in democratized digital waqf. That is the vision ceiling Round-1 lacked.
