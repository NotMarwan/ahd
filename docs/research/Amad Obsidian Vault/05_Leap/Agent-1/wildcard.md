# 🃏 Agent 1 Wildcard — أمانة Amana · the agentic trust primitive
**Out-of-lane** (Lane 4 — *A New Primitive*; parked for portfolio range). **Track:** 5/2 · **Req:** /03 CX + /02 AI.

## One-liner
**"Amana is autonomous escrow for the millions of informal, trust-based deals Saudis do every day — selling a used car, paying a freelancer, splitting a group purchase: an AI agent holds the money in *amana* (trust), verifies the agreed condition, and releases it the instant both sides are satisfied — no riba, no middleman, no 'I'll transfer after you deliver' standoff."**

## Why it could be a leap
- **A new primitive, not an app feature:** today the informal economy runs on *risky trust* ("you send first" / "no, you deliver first"). Amana inserts an **autonomous, neutral agent** that holds funds in **wadiʿah/amana** (a Shariah-clean deposit-in-trust — no interest, no gharar when terms are explicit) and releases on a verified condition. It's a **trust rail for P2P money** that didn't exist.
- **Frontier × reality:** PIS (hold + release) + ALLaM (parse the agreed terms in Arabic) + Nafath (identity of both parties) — all 2026.
- **Emotion:** removes the small, constant anxiety of being cheated in everyday deals — dignity + safety for ordinary transactions.
- **Inevitability:** once you see "an agent that holds the money until the deal is done," informal cash/transfer standoffs feel primitive.

## Why it's NOT my champion
Narrower emotional ceiling than [[concept-wakeel|Wakeel]] (transactional trust vs. lifelong duty), and the two-sided condition-verification is fiddly to demo cleanly in 72h. Strong as a **new-primitive** entry — flagged for **Agent 4** to take or kill without colliding with my Agentic-Money lane or Agent 3's faith lane.

## Distinct-from-kill-list
No credit/lending (it holds the buyer's *own* money), no score, no game, no fraud-interceptor. A trust/escrow primitive, new.
