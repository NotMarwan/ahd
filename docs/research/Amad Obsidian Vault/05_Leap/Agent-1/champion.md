# 👑 Agent 1 Champion — [[concept-wakeel|وكيل Wakeel]] (87/100)
> Crowned only after surviving its own red team.

## Why it's a LEAP, not an iteration (one sentence)
The world is racing to invent a trust framework so AI agents can move money — Wakeel reveals that **Islam already wrote that framework 1,400 years ago (wakala)**, and points the resulting agent not at *optimizing spending* (everyone's doing that) but at *fulfilling sacred duties* (no one is) — a category only an Islamic bank could create and the West literally cannot copy.

## 🔴 RED TEAM — attack before crowning

### Per-criterion: "why might judges score this LOW?"
1. **Innovation (19):** *Attack* — "Agentic money isn't new — Cleo Autopilot exists." → *Answer:* Cleo *optimizes an individual's spending*; Wakeel *fulfils religious/family obligations under an Islamic agency contract*. The wakala-governance + duty-fulfilment framing is unseen. **Lead the pitch on the wakala reveal + the mother-support moment, not on "an AI agent."**
2. **Technical (16):** *Attack* — "The 'autonomy' is just scheduled transfers with a wrapper." → *Honest:* partly fair for 72h. *Fix:* show the **guardrail engine genuinely deciding** — sequencing duties, detecting a shortfall, reshuffling to protect mom's transfer, escalating an out-of-scope request live. That's real agent logic, not a cron job. Disclose ALLaM-as-seam honestly.
3. **Data (16):** *Attack* — "Acting ≠ analysing; where's the data depth?" → *Fix:* the **obligation-sequencing/affordability engine** is the data story — affordability, shortfall prediction, fair reshuffle, zakat accrual — shown as live decisions. (This is the weakest criterion; over-invest the demo here.)
4. **UX (14):** *Attack* — "An app that moves your money could feel scary, not delightful." → *Fix:* the **wakala grant** (you stay in control, scope is explicit) + the **Wakil's Ledger** (every duty kept, beautifully shown) turn fear into *pride and relief*. Calm, Arabic-first, never alarming.
5. **Feasibility (22):** *Attack* — "No regulator lets an AI autonomously move customer money yet." → *Answer:* **that's exactly why wakala matters** — scoped, revocable, permissible-only, human-in-the-loop *by contract*, full audit. Frame production as phased (notify → approve → auto-within-scope). The objection is the pitch.

### Single most likely reason it loses
**It pattern-matches as "an AI assistant" in the first 15 seconds and the wakala leap never lands.** → Mitigation: the demo opens on **the grant of a wakil** and **the mother's support going out autonomously**, and says out loud: *"the West is inventing trust protocols for AI agents; Islam has had one for 1,400 years."*

### What a skeptical bank exec objects to in the first 30 seconds
*"What's our liability when an autonomous agent moves money wrongly?"* → Built into the design: **bounded scope + revocability + human-in-the-loop + amana audit trail** — the wakala conditions ARE the liability controls. Wakeel reduces unmanaged risk vs. a customer fumbling manual transfers.

### "Is the wow real, or am I impressed by my own framing?"
Honest check: the *idea* is genuinely novel (wakala-governed agentic money + duty-fulfilment — verified nobody is doing this). The *risk* is execution theatre — if the demo is just scheduled transfers, the wow evaporates. **So the build MUST show the agent deciding/reshuffling/escalating, not just paying.** The wow is real **only if** the autonomy is visible.

### What breaks in the live demo + fallback
- PIS sandbox/ALLaM flakiness → **fully offline, deterministic seeded persona + pre-recorded run**; nothing depends on a live call.
- The agent does something unexpected on stage → fixed demo personas with vetted mandate outcomes; "explore mode" only on safe scopes.

### Fixed vs disclosed
- **Fixed:** demo opens on the wakala grant + mother-support; over-invest the sequencing/decision visuals (data); calm reassuring UX; liability framed as the wakala controls.
- **Disclosed honestly:** ALLaM is a seam (deterministic) in 72h; rails are sandbox/synthetic; the "autonomy" is scope-bounded tool-calling (which is *also* the Shariah-faithful design — a wakil acts only within granted authority).

**Verdict:** survives — the fixes are inside 72h, and the central idea withstands attack. **Crowned.**
