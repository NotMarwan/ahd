# 🏦 AMAD 2026 — Hackathon Brief
*(Authoritative summary; full scrape in `../AMAD_HACKATHON_2026_FULL_DOSSIER.md`)*

**What:** Fintech hackathon — build a digital solution for the **Saudi financial sector**.
**Partners:** **Alinma Bank** (Shariah-compliant / Islamic bank) × **Tuwaiq Academy**.
**Where/when:** 72h onsite, Tuwaiq Academy HQ, Riyadh. **16–18 July 2026.** Enrichment 5–16 July (hybrid). **Registration closes 26 June 2026** (initial idea + team of 3–5).
**Prizes:** 1st = **250,000 SAR**, 2nd = 150,000, 3rd = 100,000 (500k total).
**Final judging:** live **pitch + demo** to a panel on 18 July. Deliverable = working demo + deck.

## The 6 tracks (pick ONE per concept)
1. Generative AI for FinTech · 2. Customer Experience · 3. Financial Regulations (RegTech) · 4. Financial Education · 5. Open Banking · 6. Gamification

## The 4 requirements (satisfy ≥1)

## The 5 evaluation criteria (THE RUBRIC)
1. Innovation & creativity · 2. Technical implementation · 3. Data analysis · 4. UX · 5. Real-world feasibility in finance

**Strategic read:** Optimize for "**Alinma could ship this next quarter**" (criterion 5 — hardest to fake). /01 Data + /02 AI together cover 3 of 5 criteria.
