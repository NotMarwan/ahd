# 👑 Rushd v2 — sharpened after the competitive audit
> Supersedes [[concept-rushd-shariah-copilot]] (89). Re-judged **92/100** after reading the bar: [[concept-haseen|Haseen 90]] (A2) and [[concept-namaa|Namaa 88]] (A4). This file is the improvement + re-judging pass; the v1 brief stays as the record.

## Why v1 wasn't enough (honest audit)
Reading Haseen exposed three real gaps in v1 Rushd:
1. **It read as a "sprawling assistant."** v1 listed tag + purify + act + advise — broad. Judges reward *focus* (anti-pattern: over-scope). Haseen is laser-focused on one moment; Rushd needed one spine.
2. **No memorable artifact.** Haseen has "stop the scam"; Namaa has "Premium Bonds, halal." v1 Rushd had a *category* ("conscience layer"), not a *thing* a tired judge repeats.
3. **Soft data score (16) and a live competitor (Malaa) already does auto-zakat** — v1 didn't differentiate against Malaa, risking the "differentiated" gate.

## The fix: ONE spine — the **Tayyib Score** (مؤشر الطيّب)
> *Tayyib (طيّب)* = pure/wholesome — the Qur'anic quality of lawful wealth ("كلوا من طيّبات ما رزقناكم"). The score measures **the money, not the person's faith** — a transparency tool, never a judgment of piety. This framing is the guardrail.

Rushd becomes **one product with one number**: a live, explainable **Tayyib Score** — how Shariah-clean your money is right now — backed by a **Shariah ledger** that does three things and only three things:
1. **Classify + explain (the score).** Every income source, holding, and transaction is scored compliant / doubtful / non-compliant, rolled into a single 0–100 Tayyib Score with a **tap-to-see-why** citing a board ruling. *(This is the data-depth fix: a real composite index with category × time drill-down.)*
2. **Zakat, always-on (the visceral moment).** The ledger continuously computes **zakat owed right now** (nisab + hawl-aware, ZATCA Zakaty methodology) and sets it aside — turning the dreaded year-end scramble into "your zakat today is **SAR 4,212**, already reserved, one tap to pay."
3. **Purify automatically (the uniquely-Islamic wow).** Incidental non-compliant income (erroneous interest, a doubtful refund) is detected, quarantined, and routed to charity with one-tap consent — *"we found and purified SAR 340 this year for you."*

**Agentic actions are reframed** — not "an assistant that does everything," but **"the moves that raise your Tayyib Score"**: shift surplus to a Murabaha pot, switch a doubtful holding to a compliant one, set up halal finance. Action is *in service of the one number*, which kills the sprawl perception.

## The memorable artifact (the hour-70 stickiness)
**"A credit score, but for how halal your money is — computed live, explained with citations, and Alinma fixes it for you."** A judge repeats *"the halal score that pays your zakat and purifies your money automatically."* That's the thing v1 lacked.

## Differentiation v2 (incl. the live competitors I missed)
| Competitor | What they do | Why Rushd v2 wins |
|---|---|---|
| **Malaa** (Shariah PFM, auto-zakat) | Auto-zakat on aggregated data; third-party app | Rushd is **the bank's own layer** → can *act* on products + *purify at source* + cite *Alinma's own Shariah board* (human-in-loop). An aggregator can compute zakat; it can't move your money halal or own the fatwa trail. |
| **Zakaty / Zoya / Zaqat** | Standalone zakat/purification calculators | Destinations you visit; Rushd is **ambient + continuous + action-taking**, with one score. |
| **Al Rajhi "Rajhi" / chatbots** | Q&A | Rushd doesn't answer — it **scores, explains with citations, and fixes**. |
| **Robo-advisors (Derayah, Abyan)** | Purify *investment portfolios* | Rushd governs **all money + behavior + products**, conversationally; not a regulated investment advisor. |

**Unfair edge (unchanged, now sharper):** only an Islamic bank with a Shariah board can own a *trusted, action-taking* Tayyib Score. Conventional banks can't; aggregators (Malaa) can compute but can't act or own the rulings.

## Feasibility hooks added from team intel
- **Channel:** ships natively into the Alinma app, or into **"iz"** (youth) / **"iz Business"** (Oct-2025, freelancers/SMEs) — the exact engaged segments. *(Inherited from Agent 4's terrain.)*

- **Licensing lane:** scope stays "Shariah classification + zakat + education + execution of Alinma's own products" → clear of CMA robo-advisory licensing; AI never issues fatwa (human board) → clean SAMA/PDPL governance.

## Updated red-team (what changed)
- **New attack — "Malaa already does this."** → Answer: Malaa computes; Rushd *acts + purifies at source + owns the board rulings* as the bank. Say this out loud in the pitch.
- **New attack — "A 'halal score' judges people's faith → reputational landmine."** → Answer (critical): the score measures **the money's compliance, not the person**; framing + tone are non-negotiable ("here's what needs attention," never "you are sinful"). Shariah-board-reviewed wording.
- **Persisting attack — "looks like a chatbot."** → Now mitigated structurally: the product opens on the **score + zakat-due + purified-this-year**, not a chat box. Chat is a secondary surface.
