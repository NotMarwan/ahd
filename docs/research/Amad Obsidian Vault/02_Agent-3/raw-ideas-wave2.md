## Fusion tests (productive overlap — does combining beat Rushd v2?)
- **Fusion A — Rushd × Namaa (conscience + hibah rewards):** "raise your Tayyib Score → earn hibah → grow deposits." Powerful, but **collides with Agent 4's owned moat (Namaa)** → reject for portfolio hygiene; flag as a *synthesis-stage* combined product, not my champion.
- **Fusion B — Rushd × Haseen (conscience + scam interception):** the layer also blocks haram/fraudulent payments. Compelling "trust + protection," but **fraud is Agent 2's owned space** → reject; flag for synthesis as the eventual "Alinma trust layer."
- **Fusion C — Rushd × Rizq (Tayyib conscience for freelancers, in iz Business):** auto zakat+VAT+purification on irregular income + halal buffer + a freelancer Tayyib Score. **In-lane, no external collision.** Genuinely interesting: gains *focus + the underserved narrative*, loses *mass-market identity*. Net ~88–90. **Strategic variant, not a replacement** — keep Rushd mass-market as champion; offer "Rushd for iz Business" as the segment cut. (Note: overlaps my own [[concept-rizq-freelancer-copilot|Rizq]] + Agent 4's [[concept-misnad|Misnad]] — don't pitch all three.)

## Judging verdict (honest)
**Rushd v2 (the Tayyib Score) holds as champion.** Nothing in wave 2 beats it because the best new ideas are either:
1. **Capabilities of Rushd** (#19 checkout verdict, #20 contract-checker, #21 second-opinion, #23 voice, #28 zakat-stream) → they *strengthen* Rushd, they don't replace it; or
2. **Fusions that collide** with other agents' owned moats (A, B); or
3. **Too narrow or too big** for 72h (#24–27).

The rare quad Rushd v2 still owns alone: **uncopyable moat + a memorable artifact (the score) + a visceral quantified moment (zakat/purification in riyals) + real data depth + mass-market + clean feasibility.**

## Roadmap folds (added to Rushd's product story, no new scope risk)
- **v1 demo:** Tayyib Score + classify/explain + purification + zakat + Murabaha action (already specced).
- **Fast-follow capabilities (mention in the deck as roadmap):** contract-checker (#20), checkout verdict (#19), product second-opinion (#21), voice mode (#23).
- **Segment cut:** "Rushd for iz Business" (#Fusion C) for freelancers.
