## Converge — 3 advanced to full evaluation
1. **[[concept-rushd-shariah-copilot]]** (#1, absorbs #2/#13/#16) — Cluster A champion. Beats the others by **covering 4 criteria at once** (innovation, AI, UX, feasibility-as-Islamic-moat) with the most ownable wedge and the strongest "only Alinma" demo. The Shariah-conscience layer is something **no conventional bank can copy**.
2. **[[concept-rizq-freelancer-copilot]]** (#3, absorbs #14/#17) — a *different bet*: serve the **2.25M underserved freelancers** Alinma's Freelance Card targets. Best **data story** (irregular-income modeling) + sustainability/inclusion narrative. Can consume Agent 1's [[concept-tadfuq|Tadfuq]] as its credit backend (synergy).
3. **[[concept-khidma-resolution-agent]]** (#6, absorbs #7/#12) — a *different bet*: attacks the **measured −82% service sentiment** with the most bulletproof "saves the bank money" feasibility case (Monzo 90%-resolution proof). Lower innovation, highest certainty.

### Why the cuts were cut (one line each)
- #4 Sawt (voice) → strong inclusion wedge but **live-demo voice reliability is a gate risk**; folded into Rushd as a voice mode.
- #5 Basira → real, but its proactivity **overlaps Rushd**; keeping both weakens internal portfolio diversity. Folded into Rushd's proactive layer.
- #9 gamified pots → **Agent 4's lane** (gamification); low novelty here.
- #10 BNPL conscience → behavioral, **drifts to Agent 4**; folded into Rushd's spend view.
- #11 SME ZATCA×OB → **out of lane** (Lane 1) → promoted to [[wildcard]].
- #15 fraud explainer → **Agent 2 owns fraud** ([[concept-haseen|Haseen]]); avoid collision.
- #18 unbanked onboarding → highest impact, **lowest 72h feasibility** (KYC+voice+inclusion is too broad); deferred.
