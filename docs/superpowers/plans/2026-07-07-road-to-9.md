story is: **«عهد» المنتج** (the app — premium, 13 screens, the whole pitch) built on **«النسخة المرجعيّة
المختومة»** (the frozen demo — repositioned as the *sealed reference implementation*: its SHA-256 tripwire
`e2f48467…` becomes a STORY BEAT — "even our own demo is tamper-evident; we seal our software the way we

animated 9→2 collapse — the demo's crown visual — is REBUILT natively in the app (JL-3, in flight), so the
app never has to apologize on stage.
