# 📑 EVIDENCE BRIEF — Ahd (عهد)

> **What this is.** A single, honestly-graded evidence base for the Ahd pitch, consolidating the project's existing
> Arsenal + ledgers and **upgrading a handful of high-value facts with live web verification (2026-06-21)**. It does
> not invent data. Where the web confirmed or *corrected* a project claim, the row says so and links the real URL.
>
> **Ahd in one line.** An Islamic-finance prototype: a bank that **witnesses/seals** interest-free interpersonal
> loans (qard hasan) — it **never lends, judges, charges interest, or scores**. It is the *scribe* of Qur'an 2:282,
> not a lender and not a witness.
>
> **Grading legend (used in every row):**
> - 🟢 **VERIFIED** — a source I actually fetched on 2026-06-21 (URL given), or a figure verbatim in the project files (file cited). High confidence.
> - 🟡 **PROXY / SECONDARY** — plausible and defensible, but not primary-verified (non-KSA stand-in, model/estimate, or secondary outlet not traced to the primary body). Labelled why.
> - 🔴 **UNVERIFIED — needs counsel / field confirmation** — no clean source found, or the claim is inherently a judge's/board's/regulator's call. This is the honest default where I could not confirm.
>
> **Integrity note.** No Shariah ruling is issued here; Shariah points cite scholars / AAOIFI standards / Qur'an. Legal
> article numbers are reported only where a real source supports them; otherwise they are flagged 🔴 for counsel.

The law has **129 articles, issued 30 Dec 2021, in force 7 Jul 2022**. *Exact* article numbers for "digital evidence = documentary" and "e-signature" are still **not pinned to a primary source** I could read → still 🔴 (OT-CITE).
