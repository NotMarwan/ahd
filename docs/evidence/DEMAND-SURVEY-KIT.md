# 🧪 DEMAND-RESEARCH KIT — Ahd (عهد)

> **What this is.** A ready-to-run field-research instrument whose single job is to **close OT-A1 / D-9** — the
> project's #1 evidence gap. The Evidence Brief grades the thesis in two halves: the *documentation-habit / scale*
> half is well-sourced (🟢, court data: promissory notes = 58.6% of enforcement requests), but the **RELATIONAL
> half** — *that Saudis find asking-for-their-money-back awkward and would value a neutral witness* — has **zero
> Saudi-primary evidence** (🔴). This kit is the instrument to acquire that evidence honestly.
>
> **What this is NOT.** It is not data. It contains no findings, no percentages-as-results, no "Saudis told us…".
> It is the survey, the interview guide, and the method. **You run it; it produces the evidence.** Until then, the
> honest line stays the one in Q-A1: *"the court data is hard Saudi proof; the warmth evidence is the interviews we
> are running."*
>
> **Design north star (read before editing any question).** The fatal failure mode of demand research for a product
> you already love is **leading the witness** — asking "would you use a neutral witness for your loans?" and
> celebrating the yes. This kit refuses that. It measures **behaviour** (did you lend? did you chase? did you get
> repaid?) and **felt pain** (how did asking feel?) **without ever naming Ahd, a bank, an app, or a witness** until
> the very last block. At least one item is engineered so a *high* score **disconfirms** the thesis. Intellectual
> honesty is the whole point — a gap closed with a leading instrument is not closed.

## 1 · GOAL & HYPOTHESES — the precise claims under test (pre-registered)

**Goal.** Produce the first KSA-primary read on the *relational* demand thesis, to a standard a hostile judge accepts:
behaviour-frequency, felt awkwardness/relationship-strain, willingness for a neutral reminder, and the role of piety.
Pre-registering the validate/invalidate thresholds **before** data collection is what makes a "yes" credible and a
"no" survivable — we commit to reporting the result either way.

**Decision rule (defined up front).** OT-A1 is declared **CLOSED-POSITIVE** only if **H2 AND H3 both pass** on a
sample meeting the §4 quality bar (n ≥ 150 survey + ≥ 15 interviews, screened Saudi residents, channel-diversified).
H1 establishes the population denominator (it is mostly a re-confirmation of the 🟢 scale story at the *interpersonal*
level). H4 is a *modifier* — it shapes positioning, not the go/no-go. If H2 or H3 fails, OT-A1 is **CLOSED-NEGATIVE**:
that is a real finding the team reports honestly, pivoting the pitch's emotional half to the verified scale story.

> **Placeholder discipline.** Targets below are written as `X` / `N` *on purpose*. They are the thresholds the
> fieldwork must clear, **not** measured values. Do not paste a number here that did not come from a run of this kit.

| # | Hypothesis (the claim) | Primary item(s) | VALIDATES the thesis if… | INVALIDATES (or weakens) if… |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **H1** | **Lending is a live, recent, near-universal behaviour.** A large majority of Saudi adults have lent money to family/friends in the past 12 months. | Q4, Q5, Q6 | ≥ **X%** (pre-set target: a clear majority, e.g. ≥60%) lent at least once in 12 months; a meaningful share lent amounts that *matter* (not only trivial sums). | < a majority lent, **or** lending is overwhelmingly trivial (<200 SAR) and rare → the "national-scale interpersonal money" framing is thinner than the court data implies. |
| **H2** | **Asking for repayment is socially painful / relationship-straining.** A majority who have been owed find *asking for their money back* awkward, and a meaningful share say money has *strained or cost a relationship*. | Q7, Q8, Q9, Q11 | A majority rate asking as awkward/uncomfortable (top-2 box) **AND** ≥ **X%** report avoiding/delaying the ask because of the relationship **AND** a non-trivial share (≥ **X%**) report an actual strain/loss. | A majority say asking is *easy/no problem* → **the core relational premise of Ahd is wrong**; the pain Ahd removes does not widely exist. This is the disconfirmation that most threatens the thesis (see §1 note). |
| **H3** | **A neutral third-party record/reminder is wanted.** A meaningful segment would value a *neutral, non-judgmental* way to keep the record and prompt repayment — measured via behaviour-gap and a neutral-channel preference, **not** "would you use our app." | Q10, Q12, Q13 | ≥ **X%** (pre-set: a meaningful segment, e.g. ≥40%) both (a) currently keep *no_/weak_ record and (b) prefer a neutral/automatic reminder over confronting the person themselves. | The dominant preference is "handle it myself, person-to-person" / "a third party would be intrusive or shameful" → the *witness/reminder* wedge has weak pull even if pain (H2) exists. |
| **H4** | **Piety / riba-anxiety shapes lending behaviour** (a positioning modifier, not a go/no-go). | Q14, Q15 | A meaningful share say avoiding *riba* and obeying the duty-to-write-down (2:282) shapes how they lend / whether they'd document → the Islamic-finance framing is a *distribution asset*, not a niche limiter. | Piety is reported as irrelevant to how they lend → keep the Shariah layer as compliance scaffolding, not as the emotional hook; lead on dignity/convenience instead. |

**The single most dangerous disconfirmation.** **H2.** If a representative Saudi sample says *asking for your money
back is easy and doesn't hurt the relationship*, the relational thesis — the reason Ahd is a *warmth* product and not
just a fancier سند — collapses, and re-sourcing scale (D-1…D-6) cannot rescue it. H3 and H4 are about *shape and reach*;
H2 is about whether the **wound Ahd exists to dress is real**. The instrument therefore over-invests in measuring H2
cleanly and from multiple angles (behavioural Q8/Q10 + felt Q7/Q9 + outcome Q11), so a pass is robust and a fail is
believed.

## 2 · الاستبيان العربيّ المصغّر — Self-administered Arabic micro-survey (n ≥ 150)

> **For the team (English notes, do not show respondents).**
> - **Length:** 15 items, ~4–6 minutes. Self-administered (web form / WhatsApp link). Arabic only; colloquial-friendly.
> - **Neutrality rules baked in:** (1) the word «عهد»/Ahd, "bank", "app", and "witness/شاهد" appear **nowhere** in
>   Q1–Q15 — only in the optional post-survey block §2.1, after all measurement is done. (2) Likert scales are
>   **balanced** (symmetric positive/negative anchors, neutral midpoint). (3) No double-barrelled items (each asks
>   one thing). (4) **Q8 is the planted disconfirmer** — its "إطلاقًا، الموضوع عادي" option lets a respondent flatly
>   reject the awkwardness premise; if that option dominates, H2 fails and we report it.
> - **Branching:** Q1 screens out non-residents → end. Q5/Q7/Q11 ask "have you ever…"; Q8–Q10 are framed for anyone
>   who has *ever been owed* (Q7), else skipped.
> - **Anti-bias order:** behaviour first (Q4–Q6), then feelings (Q7–Q9), then the willingness items (Q10, Q12, Q13)
>   late, so the "solution-shaped" questions cannot prime the earlier pain measurement.
> - **Randomise** the answer order of multi-option items (Q10, Q12, Q13) per respondent to kill order-bias; keep
>   Likert anchors fixed.

## 3 · دليل المقابلة شبه المنظَّمة — Semi-structured interview guide (15–20 people)

> **For the interviewer (English).** 25–35 min, Arabic, recorded with consent. Goal: **stories, not opinions** — the
> single real Saudi voice that "beats every US stat" (Evidence Brief, D-9). **Do not name Ahd, a bank, an app, or a
> "witness" until Block F.** Let silences sit. Probe with «وش صار بعدها؟» / «وكيف حسّيت؟» / «ليش؟». Recruit a spread
> across the two personas the project designed for — **a Naif** (irregular-income freelancer, owed by clients/friends,
> hates chasing) and **a Munira** (older family lender, lends to children/relatives with no due-date, forgives
> quietly) — plus people in between. **15 prompts**, grouped; ask the openers verbatim, follow the story.

**Block A — Warm-up & lending life (no money-pain yet).**
1. «حدّثني عن آخر مرّة أعطيت فيها أحدًا مالًا على أن يُرجعه — مين كان، وكم، وليش؟» *(let them narrate; map persona.)*
2. «كم شخصٍ تقريبًا الآن عليه لك مال، أو لك عليه؟ تقدر تعدّهم بسهولة، ولا تحتاج ترجع للواتساب؟» *(probe the "scattered in my head" reality from Naif's journey.)*

**Block B — The story of being owed (the H2 heart).**
3. «احكِ لي عن مرّةٍ أعطاك أحدٌ وعدًا بردّ مالك… وتأخّر. وش صار بالضبط؟» *(get the narrative; do not summarise for them.)*
4. «في تلك اللحظة، وش اللي قلته أو سويته؟ طالبت؟ سكتّ؟ لمّحت؟» *(behaviour — maps to Q10.)*
5. «وكيف كان شعورك وأنت تفكّر تطالبه؟ وش اللي كان يمنعك أو يصعّب عليك؟» *(felt awkwardness — the core; probe «إحراج»، «جشَع»، «خوف على العلاقة».)*
6. «هل تغيّر شيءٌ في العلاقة بينكما بعدها؟ ولو بسيط؟» *(strain/loss — maps to Q11.)*

**Block C — The other chair (being the one who owes).**
7. «وعكس الصورة — مرّةٍ كنت أنت مدينًا لأحدٍ وتأخّرت. كيف عشتها؟ وكيف ودّك الطرف الثاني يتصرّف معك؟» *(dignity-of-asking, both sides — Munira's "let my son ask with dignity, I just bless".)*

**Block D — Coping & workarounds today (reveals the unmet job).**
8. «كيف توثّق هذي الأمور عادةً — لو توثّق أصلًا؟ ورّني إذا ما عندك مانع.» *(observe real artefacts: chats, notes, nothing — maps to Q12.)*
9. «هل صار وخسرت مالًا لأنك ما تملك إثباتًا؟ احكِ لي.» *(Naif's "ghosted on 4,000, only had screenshots".)*
10. «لو احتجت تذكّر أحدًا بمالك بدون ما تكون أنت اللي يطالب — وش الطرق اللي تخطر لك؟ وش يريحك منها وش يحرجك؟» *(elicits the neutral-channel idea from THEM — H3, unprompted.)*

**Block E — Religion & the act of writing (H4, still no product).**
11. «هل لدينك أثرٌ في طريقة إقراضك — مثل الحرص على خلوّه من الفائدة، أو الإحساس إنّ توثيق الدَّين مطلوبٌ شرعًا؟» *(piety as driver; let them bring up 2:282 or riba unprompted if they will.)*
12. «بعضهم يرى كتابة الدَّين بين الأهل نوعًا من قلّة الثقة. وش رأيك أنت؟» *(surfaces the initiator-stigma — the sharpest cultural hole, red-team A6.)*

**Block F — The wish, then (only now) the concept.**
13. «لو كان في يدك أن تخترع شيئًا يحلّ هذا الموقف كلّه — وش يسوّي؟» *(let them design the solution before you show yours.)*
14. *(Now, for the first time, describe the neutral concept in one plain sentence — no brand, no "bank":)* «تخيّل خدمةً محايدةً تكتب الاتفاق وتحفظه وتذكّر بلطفٍ نيابةً عنك عند التأخّر، بلا فوائد وبلا أحكام — وش أوّل شيءٍ يخطر ببالك؟ وش يطمئنك ووش يقلقك فيها؟» *(capture objections AND pull.)*
15. «لو كانت من بنكٍ إسلاميّ تثق به — هل يغيّر هذا شعورك؟ ليش؟» *(only at the very end: does the Islamic-bank frame add trust or add weirdness — informs Q-A2 positioning, not H-go/no-go.)*

> **Interviewer discipline.** If a participant reaches for praise of the concept early, redirect to story: «قبل لا
> نوصل للفكرة — رجّعني لمرّةٍ حقيقيّة صارت لك». The deliverable from each interview is **one verbatim quote + the
> behaviour + the feeling**, not a thumbs-up.

## 4 · METHODOLOGY — sampling, bias controls, ethics, analysis

### 4.1 Sampling & channels (a credible Saudi sample, cheaply, pre-pitch)
- **Target n:** ≥ **150** completed surveys (after screen-outs) + **15–20** interviews. This is a *directional,
  pitch-grade* sample, **not** a nationally representative one — say so out loud (see §4.5). It is enough to move D-9
  from "zero evidence" to "first KSA-primary read."
- **Quota frame (soft, to avoid a monoculture):** aim for a spread, not a single bubble — both genders; the two
  persona poles **(a) young irregular-income earners / freelancers (Naif)** and **(b) 40+ family lenders (Munira)**;
  and a mix of regions/cities. Track quotas live; if one cell dominates, push recruitment to the thin cells.
- **Channels (cheap, pre-pitch):** (1) WhatsApp broadcast / family-and-friends snowball seeded from **multiple
  unrelated starting nodes** (not one network); (2) X/Twitter + relevant Saudi subreddits/forums; (3) freelancer
  communities and small-business groups (reaches Naif); (4) a few in-person intercepts for the older, less-app-savvy
  Munira segment who won't fill a web form — **read the survey aloud** for them (a phone-administered mode).
- **Interview recruitment:** purposive — explicitly recruit *at least* 4–5 "Naif" profiles and 4–5 "Munira" profiles
  from the survey tail (Q-end opt-in: «هل تقبل مقابلةً قصيرةً لاحقًا؟»), plus walk-in variety.

### 4.2 Bias controls (the credibility of a "yes" depends on these)
- **No-naming firewall:** Ahd / bank / app / witness appear nowhere in Q1–Q15 or interview Blocks A–E. This is the
  single most important control against demand-inflation.
- **Behaviour-before-attitude order:** frequency (Q4–Q6) precedes feelings (Q7–Q11) precedes willingness (Q12–Q13);
  the concept reaction is **quarantined post-submission** (§2.1).
- **A planted disconfirmer:** Q8's "سهلٌ تمامًا… لا يحرجني إطلاقًا" and Q13's "أفضّل ألّا يُذكَّر / أذكّره بنفسي" give
  respondents a clean, socially-easy way to **reject** the thesis. If those win, we report a fail.
- **Balanced Likert + neutral midpoints** on every agree/disagree item (Q9, Q14, Q15) to avoid acquiescence pull.
- **Randomised option order** on Q10/Q12/Q13 to kill primacy bias.
- **Self-administered + anonymous** to reduce social-desirability on the awkwardness items; interviews probe *past
  behaviour* ("what did you actually do") over hypotheticals.
- **Interviewer non-leading script** (§3 discipline note); record verbatim, don't paraphrase into the product.
- **Pre-registration:** the §1 thresholds and decision rule are fixed **before** collection; report H2/H3 against them
  regardless of outcome. No moving the goalposts after seeing the data.

### 4.3 Consent & ethics
- **Opening consent line (survey):** «هذا استبيانٌ بحثيٌّ مجهولٌ للأغراض الدراسيّة، يستغرق ٥ دقائق، ولك أن تتوقّف في
  أيّ لحظة. لن نطلب اسمك ولا أرقام حساباتك. بإكمالك الاستبيان توافق على استخدام إجاباتك مجمّعةً ومجهولة.»
- **Interview consent:** verbal, recorded at the top of the tape — purpose, right to stop/skip, recording permission,
  anonymisation of names in any quote used. **Get explicit permission before quoting** any line in the deck.
- **Data minimisation (PDPL-aligned, per L-9):** collect **no** national IDs, account numbers, or contact details
  inside the survey body; store the optional interview-opt-in contact separately from responses; aggregate before
  reporting; delete raw contacts after recruitment. No financial-account data is ever requested.
- **Sensitivity:** money + family is a tender topic — Q11 and interview Block B/C must allow «أفضّل عدم الإجابة» and a
  graceful skip; never press a participant on a painful loss.

### 4.4 Analysis plan (what to tabulate, and the threshold that "closes" OT-A1)
**Tabulate (survey):**
- **H1:** % who lent ≥1× in 12 mo (Q4); distribution of typical amount (Q6) → is interpersonal lending live *and* non-trivial.
- **H2 (primary):** top-2-box % on Q8 (awkward) and Q9 (relationship fear); % on Q11 reporting any strain/loss;
  cross-tab Q8×Q10 (does felt awkwardness predict avoidant behaviour — silence/hint/forgive rather than ask).
- **H3:** among low-documenters (Q12 = "لا أوثّق"/"واتساب"), the % who on Q13 prefer the **automatic-neutral** or
  **trusted-third-party** reminder over "أذكّره بنفسي" → the size of the witness/reminder-wanting segment.
- **H4:** agree-% on Q14 (riba-avoidance) and Q15 (writing-down reassurance); cross-tab with H2/H3 to see if pious
  lenders are the warmest segment.
- **Segment cuts:** run H2/H3 separately for the **Naif cell** (young/freelancer) and the **Munira cell** (45+),
  since the personas predict *different* pains (Naif: chasing clients; Munira: no-due-date forgiveness).
- **Quarantined §2.1 concept item:** report separately, labelled exploratory; never as the proof.

**Synthesise (interviews):** thematic-code for (a) felt awkwardness language, (b) avoidance/workaround behaviours,
(c) spontaneous mention of a neutral channel (Block D/F), (d) initiator-stigma (Block E Q12), (e) piety-as-driver.
Pull **3–5 verbatim quotes** that a judge can feel.

**The closing threshold (decision rule, restated):** OT-A1 is **CLOSED-POSITIVE** iff, on a §4-quality sample,
**H2 passes** (majority find asking awkward *and* a meaningful share report avoidance/strain) **AND H3 passes** (a
meaningful low-documenter segment prefers a neutral/automatic reminder over self-confrontation), **corroborated by**
≥ 3 interview quotes naming the pain unprompted. If either fails → **CLOSED-NEGATIVE**, reported honestly, with the
pitch's emotional half re-anchored on the verified scale story (D-1…D-6) and the relational claim dropped or softened.

### 4.5 What this study can and cannot claim (state on the slide)
- ✅ Can claim: *"first KSA-primary, behaviour-based read on interpersonal-lending pain (n≈…)."*
- ❌ Cannot claim: national representativeness, causal effect, or market size. It is **directional pitch-grade
  evidence**, not a census — pairing it with the 🟢 court data (the hard, representative half) is what makes the
  demand slide whole.

## 5 · HOW TO PRESENT THE RESULT (one paragraph)

A **closed-positive** OT-A1 plugs straight into the gap the Evidence Brief flags as the round-decider. On **deck
slide 2 (the problem)**, the relational claim graduates from a *labelled US-proxy footnote* (D-8) to a **Saudi-primary
headline**: keep the hard 🟢 court datum as the spine — *"promissory notes are 58.6% of the Kingdom's enforcement
requests"* — and add, beneath it, the new field line — *"and in our own Saudi field study (n≈150 + 18 interviews),
**X%** found asking for their money back awkward, **X%** had delayed or avoided the ask to protect a relationship, and
a low-documenting segment preferred a neutral reminder to confronting the person"* — sourced to *this* kit, with one
verbatim quote (e.g., Naif's *"كل فلوسي عند الناس، وأنا أكره أطالب"* rendered as a real respondent's words) as the
emotional anchor. In the war-room, this **upgrades Q-A1's 🔴 tail to 🟢**: the honest concession *"the warmth evidence
is the interviews we are running"* becomes *"the warmth evidence is the Saudi field study we ran — here are the numbers
and a Saudi voice,"* removing the single most dangerous attack's last soft spot. If instead OT-A1 closes **negative**,
present *that* with the same candor — *"we tested the relational premise on Saudis and it was weaker than expected; our
demand case stands on the verified national-scale documentation behaviour, and we've re-scoped the emotional framing
accordingly"* — which, per the playbook's first rule (*never present a 🔴 as a 🟢; candor scores*), still wins the room
on integrity rather than losing it on a bluff.
