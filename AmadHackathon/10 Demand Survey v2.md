# Demand Survey v2

Status: written specification approved; implementation-ready TDD plan complete. Survey code has not been changed yet.

- Goal: judge-grade directional evidence first; product learning second.
- Length: 9 questions on the normal path and 12 after delayed repayment.
- Order: consent, eligibility, behavior, pain, current workaround, preferred solution, then one exploratory product question.
- Corrections: forgiveness is not avoidance; low-documentation includes WhatsApp, personal notes, and no documentation.
- Delivery: a new Google Form with a private linked Sheet; public links only are tracked.
- Recruitment: pretest 5–8, soft launch 20, minimum 80, target 150, normal stop 250.
