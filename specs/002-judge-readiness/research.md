# Research: Judge Readiness

## Decision: Keep canned tamper in the timed cold open

**Rationale**: Current script is at its word and time ceiling. Manual judge entry introduces latency and typo
risk. The built manual field remains stronger in questions or the extended path.  
**Alternatives considered**: Manual typing by default; remove manual path. Both rejected.

## Decision: One data insight, two evidence classes

**Rationale**: The measured Findex prevalence demonstrates real context; the deterministic sensitivity band
demonstrates product analysis. Combining them is memorable only when labels remain adjacent.  
**Alternatives considered**: More statistics; synthetic projection alone. Rejected for density and credibility.

## Decision: Font is an asset gate, not a blocker

**Rationale**: An approved OFL Arabic font improves distinctiveness, but an unlicensed download creates release
risk. Existing system typography can still be simplified and enlarged.  
**Alternatives considered**: Download any popular font; postpone all typography. Rejected.

## Decision: End on proof or mercy

**Rationale**: Returning to the home screen weakens recency. Holding a verified seal or mercy-first settlement
anchors the closing sentence.  
**Alternatives considered**: Logo-only close; home screen close. Rejected after reviewer feedback.
