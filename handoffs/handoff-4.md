## 6. Key decisions + rationale (so you don't re-litigate)
1. **Champion = Namaa, not Faheem/Misnad.** Namaa wins the highest-weighted criterion (feasibility 25) with a **direct CASA/P&L line**; Faheem ("education") reads as soft revenue; Misnad's segment is narrower AND overlaps Agent 3's Rizq.
2. **The Shariah reward engine is the moat — design it zero-chance.** Prize-linked savings (Premium Bonds/Long Game) is **haram (maysir)**. ADIB **Ghina** ships a halal prize account but via a **contested chance draw**; **Saudi scholarship is stricter**. Namaa uses **guaranteed hibah + skill + merchant-funded cashback, NO draw** → clears the strict bar. This is the un-clonable edge — never revert to a lottery mechanic.
3. **Do NOT rebuild** Hakbah (group savings, 1.3M users), Cashee/ZakiPay (teen cards), Drahim/Malaa (PFM), STC Bank (round-up), or Alinma Fantasy (sports prediction). White space = **individual, Shariah-native, behavior-change saving + contextual literacy**.
4. **v2 made the AI agentic** (capacity → next-best-nudge → propose/approve/**execute**) to ride the 2026 frontier — but **demo human-in-the-loop** (agent only moves money INTO the user's own savings; one-tap approve) to keep the 72h gate + the consent/safety story clean.
5. **Wildcard = out-of-lane Open Banking (Malaa'a),** deliberately chosen to avoid colliding with Agent 2's anti-scam space and Hakbah's group-savings turf.
