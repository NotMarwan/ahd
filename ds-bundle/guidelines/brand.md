# عهد — brand guidelines (for slides and new screens)

- **Soul line:** «كلمتك محفوظة، وعلاقتك محميّة» — the closing line of everything.
- **Category line:** «بنكٌ يَشهد ولا يُقرض» — say it early, verbatim.
- **Palette:** warm cream bg + deep teal (action) + gold (covenant/bank) + amber (lateness) + reserved brick-red (tamper only). Dark sealed-document green (`--seal-bg`) is the signature "proof" surface.
- **Wordmark:** gold-gradient «عَهد» on the dark hero card (`.hero .brand`) — never flat yellow, never an image.
- **Typography:** Arabic-first RTL; hashes/commands in monospace LTR blocks.
- **Numbers on slides:** the gate banner count is recomputed from `cd tests && node run-all.cjs` at build time — never copied from an old doc.
- **Honesty ceiling:** simulated rails say «محاكاة»; pending approvals say «بانتظار الهيئة»; graded stats keep their vintage labels («بيانات ٢٠٢٠–٢١»).
