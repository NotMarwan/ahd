# TrustBand — إشارة الثقة والصافي

- `.selfband` — the OWN-history trust mirror: a gold-soft band holding a 3-band WORD («وفّى بعهوده»), never a number, never a ranking. Spine rule: no numeric trust signal may ever render — no percentages, no scores, no stars.
- `.net.lak` (teal, you are owed) / `.net.alayk` (gold, you owe) / `.net.bal` (muted, balanced) — the net-balance pill. Amounts are integer SAR with grouping.
- Direction of money is color-semantic: teal=incoming right, gold=obligation, amber=late (never red).
