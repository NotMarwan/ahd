# Hero — بطاقة العلامة

Dark-green hero card with the gold-gradient wordmark and a CSS-geometry watermark (no image assets).

- Wrapper: `<div class="hero">` inside `#app` (RTL). Children: `.brand` (wordmark), `.tag` (soul line), `.sub` (supporting line).
- The watermark and gold inner ring come from `.hero` itself — never re-create them with images.
- Use once per surface (top of a screen or a title slide). Gold is semantic (bank/covenant), not decoration.
